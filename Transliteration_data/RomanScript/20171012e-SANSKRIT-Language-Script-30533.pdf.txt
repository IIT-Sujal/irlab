rASTrapatiH

rASTrapatiH rAmanAthakobindaH proktavAn yat rASTranirmANe yuvajanAnAM mahattvapUrNa-bhUmikA vartate|
rAjadhAnyAM rASTrapatibhavane samAyojite rAjyapAlAnAM sammelane shrIkovindena bhaNitaM yat deshasya bhaviSyam yUnAM
kSamatA-naitika mUlya-saMvedanAshIlatAbhiH nirmIyate| rAjyapAlAH rAjyAnAM vikAsakAryakrameSu diD.nirdeshaM pradAtuM
samarthAH ityapi rASTrapatinA pratipAditam|


himAchla pradeshaH

himAchla pradeshe vidhAnasabhA -rnivAchnasya kAryakramaH udghoSitaH| rAjye matadAnam AgAmi mAse navame
dinAMke bhavitA| etadudvoSayanA mukhya nirvAchnAyuktena e.ke. jotinA bhaNitaM yat rAjye tatkAla prabhAveNa
Adarsha-AchAra-saMhitA prabhAvI jAtA| nAmAMkanAdhisUchnA somavAsare prakAshitA bhavitA|

jeTalI

vittamantriNA arUNa jeTalinA proktaM yat vastu sevA kara pariSada bhUsampadAM karArhatAM vichArayiSyati|
amerikAyAH hArvarDa vishvavidyAlaye bhArate kara-pariSkAra-sambaddha vyAkhyAne shrIjeTalinA uktaM yat bhUsampat
kSetre sarvAdhika-karachauryaM bhavati|

smRtiH

vastramantriNA smRti IrAnI varyayA adya greTara naueDAyAM chtushchtvArishaMt bhAratIya-hastashilpa-upahAra
melakam udghATitam| melakamidaM somavAsaraM yAvat prachliSyati| chturdasha vastushreNiSu ekashatadeshebhyaH tri-
sahasrAdhikAH hastashilpinaH atra bhAgamAvahanti | melakasya AyojanaM hastashilpa-saMvardhana-pariSada vihitam|
mantriNA bhaNitaM yat tadIya mantrAlayena udyogAn samunnetuM sArthakopAyAH vihitAH, yeSu vidhika-prakriyAgata-
samarthanamapi sammilitaH|

uchchtama nyAyAlayaH

sarvochch nyAyAlayena adhivaktRNAM kRte variSTha-adhivaktA-pada-pradAnArtham aneke dik-rnideshAH
prasAritAH| eteSu pradhAnanyAyAdhIshasya adhyakSatAyAM sthAyI-samitergaThanaM pramukhaM vartate|

shrIlaMkA-dhIvarAH

shrIlaMkA nausanayA gatarAtrau paMch-bhAratIya-dhIvarAH naukayA saha nigRhItAH| nausenA pravaktrA bhaNitaM
yat‌ nigRhItAH dhIvarAH shrIlaMkAjalakSatre matsya saMgrahaM kurvanti sma|


viyatanAma-jalAplAvanam
