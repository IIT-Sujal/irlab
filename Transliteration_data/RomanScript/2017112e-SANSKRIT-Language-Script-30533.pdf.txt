mukhyavArtAH

* pradhAnamantrI narendramodI adya himAchla-pradeshasya kAgaDa़A-janapade nairvAchnikIM
janasabhA sambodhayan‌ kAgresadalam‌ AkSiptavAn‌| uktaM ch mukhyamantrI pratibhUtau
vartate para ghoSaNA-patre bhraSTAchArasya samUlanAshollikhitaH vartate|

° jammUkashmIrasya sAmbAkSatre niyantraNarekhAyAM pAkistAnasya golikAprahAreNa
sImAsurakSAbalasya eko bhaTaH vIragatiM prAptaH|

* uttarapradeshe U~chAhAra-tApavidyut‌-saMyatre durApAdite visphoTe mRtakAnAM saMkhyA triMshat‌
saMvRttA, anveSaNAya rachita-samitiH ghaTanAyAH anveSaNaM kariSyati|

pradhAnamaMtrI himAchla

pradhAnamantrI narendramodI himAchlapradeshe kAgaDA-janapade bhAjapAdalasya
nairvAchnikI janasabhA sambodhayan‌ rAjyasya mukhyamantriNa vIrabhadrasiha bhaSTAchArIH iti
avarNayat| tenoktaM yat bhraSTAchArasya bahuni prakaraNAni taM virUdhya prachlanti, paraM
kAMgresadalasya ghoSaNApatre bhraSTAchArasya samUlavinAshaH ullikhitaH asti| shrImodI
vyAharat‌ yat‌ mukhyamantrI pratibhUtau vartate ataH tena ghoSaNapatre sushAsanasya prayogaH
naiva karaNIyaH| tenoktaM yat‌ himAchlasya devabhUmi; khanana-vana-mAdakapadArtha-nividA-
sthAnAtaraNa vachkaiH vimochnIyA|

himAchla-prayAshinaH

atrAntare janatantra-saMshodhana-samityA nigaditaM yat‌ vidhAnasabhA nirvAchne
kAgresadalasya sarvAdhikapratyAshinaH koTi-patayaH, bhAjapAdalasya sarvAdhika-pratyAshinaH ch
AparAdhika-prakaraNeSu AropitAH santi| aSTaviMshaduttaratrishata-pratyAshiSu SaDuttaraikashataM
koTipatayaH bhAjapA kAMgresadalayoH vartante|

jammUkashmIra
jammUkashmIrasya sAmbAkSetre pAkistAnasya golikAprahAreNa sImA-surakSAbalasya
eko bhaTaH vIragatiM prAptaH| anantanAga-janapadasya saranAkSetre api
kendrIyarakSitArakSibalasya paMchbhaTAH AtakyAkramaNevraNitAH| AdhikArika-srotobhiH
sUchitaM yat‌ balasya pratyuttareNa AtaMkinaH palAyitAH|

uttarapradesha visphoTaH

uttarapradeshe rASTriya-tApa-vidyuta-nigamasya U~chAhArasaMyantre durApAdite visphoTe
mRtakAnAM saMkhyA triMshat‌ saMvRttA| rAjyasya pradhAnagRhasachivaH aravindakumAraH vyAharat‌
yat‌ saMkaTApannasthitau kechit‌ karmakArAH pratishataM saptatimitAH dagdhAH| nigamena
ghaTanAyAH anveSaNAdeshaH pradattaH| UrjAmantrI Ara0 ke0 siMhaH avochta yat‌ ghaTanAyA
anveSaNAya samitirekA vinirmitAsti| yA hi mAsAbhyantare anveSaNasya prativedanaM
pradAsyati| srotobhiH jApita yat‌ sAhoyyoddhArakAryANi pUrNatAgatAni|
pradhAnamantriNA hatAhatAnAM kRte sAhAyyarAshiH udghoSito'sti|

amerikA pAkistAna

amerikAyAH ekena pramukheNa sAMsadena bhaNitaM yat‌ pAkistAnAt‌ amerikAyAm‌
AgantukAnAM samyak anveSaNaM bhavitavyam| sI0sI0ena ityanena saha sAkSAtkAre
piTarakigaH idamapi avocht yat pAkistAnena sIriyAyAm islAmikasTeTa ityAtaMki
gulmasya sahAyatAyai aSTashatam‌ AtaMkinaH preSitAH santi|

pAkistAnadhIvarAH

bhAratena pAkistAnasya navadhIvarAH chtvAraH vandinaH ekaH shishuH ch adya
pratyarSitAH| videsha mantrAlayasya vijnJptauM uktaM yat gatamAse'pi dvau vandino
pratyarSitau AstAm| vijnJptau udghoSitaM yat bhArataM pAkistAnena nigaDitAnAM
bhAratIya-vandinAM dhIvarANAM ch tvaritamochnasahitAn sarvAn mAnavIyaviSayAn
atiprAthamikatAM dadAti|

shrIlaMkayA dhIvarAH nigaDitAH

shrIlaMkayAH nausenayA trayodasha-bhAratIyadhIvarAH trinaukAbhiH saha shrIlaMkIya
sAmudrika-sImAyAm‌ avaidharUpeNa matsyasagrahaNArope nigaDitAH| ete japhanAsthite
matsyavibhAge vidhika-kAryAchraNAya preSitAH santi|

hAkI

jApAnadeshe anuSThite mahilA hAki eshiyAchSaka spardhAyAM bhAratena adya
prAgupAntyachkre kajAgastAnadalaM sapta-eka iti antarAlena parAjitam‌| gurajItakauraH
triNi golakAni arjitavatI|
