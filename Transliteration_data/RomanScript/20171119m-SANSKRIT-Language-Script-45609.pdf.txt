kendrIyaH vitta sachivaH hasamukha AdhiyA bhAratIya-samavAyAn vastu-
sevAkarANAM dareSu kartanasya lAbhaM upabhoktRNAM samakSe prastotuM proktavAn| saH
udIritavAn yat upabhoktAraH navadaraM jnJAtuM vastu-sevA-kara-darAnveSaNe draSTuM
shaknuvanti |

vishva-sundarI

hariyANAyAM jAtA mAnuSI-chillareNa vishva-sudaryA-saMptadashottara
dvisahasratamakhiSTAbdasya puraskAraH vijitaH| pradhAnamantriNA narendra-modinA
mAnuSyai abhinanda pradattam|

pradhAnamantrI-avadhUtaH

pradhAnamantrI narendramodI abavrIt yat sAdhubhiH mahAtmAbhishch deshasya
saMskRtayaH prAchInakAlAt rakSitAH vartate| tena proktaM yat etAdRshaH mahAtmA
shrIraMga-avadhUta-mahArAjaH AsIt| yeSAM jIvanaM rASTrabhakteH adhyAtmanashch
saMyuktarUpaM vidyate| ahamadAbAdAt panJchSaSTyadhika dvishatakilomITara-
dUrasthe nAreshvare avadhUtamahArAjnJH panJchAshatatame nirvANa-divasopalakSye
Ayojite samArohe pradhAnamantriNA ishya-shravya-sammelanamAdhyamena bhaNitam|

rASTrapatiH rAmanAtha-kovindaH adya pUrvotarIyatrayANAM rAjyAnAM
arUNAchlapradeshasya asamasya maNipurasya ch yAtrAM vidhAsyati|
rASTrapatirUpeNa eSAM rAjyAnAM iyaM yAtrA tasya prathama yAtrA vidyate|
svIyayAtrAyAH prathame divase kovindaH iTAnagare vivekAnandakendrasya
samArohasya samApanasatre sammiliSyati| saH iTAnagare arUNAchlapradeshasya
vidhAnasabhAyAH visheSaM satramapi sambodhayiSyati|
rASTrapatiH imphAle pUrvottaravikAsasammelanasya udghATanamapi
vidhAsyati|

indirA-puraskAra-manamohana

pUrvapradhAnamantrI manamohanasiMhaH chturadhika dvisahasratamIyakhriSTAbdAt
chturdashottaradvisahasratamakhiSTAbdasya madhye deshasya netRtvaM vidhAtuM bhAratasya
vaishvika-sthitiM sudRDhIkartu asmin varSe iMdirA-gAMdhI-shAnti puraskAreNa
nirastrIkaraNa-vikAsa puraskAreNa ch sammAnito bhavitA|

veMkaiyA-nAyaDU

uparASTrapatinA ema.veMkaiyAnAyaDunA vidyArthiSu udyama-vikAsa-kendrasya
sthApanAya balaM pradattam‌| gatasAyaM saH gItama-vishvavidyAlayasya
aSTamadIkSAnta-samArohe upAdhi-pradAnAnantaraM tena idaM proktaM yat
pravidhibhiradya saMsAraH grAmarUpeNa pariNamitaH jAtaH|

dillI-mairAthana-parivahana

dillyAm‌ adya dillI hAphamairAdhana-dhAvanaM Ayojyate| atra
saptaprakArakaMdhAvanaM bhaviSyati| yatra narAH, nAryaH,variSThAH nAgarikAH
bhaviSyanti| dillI-parivahana-ArakSyanusAreNa mairAthana-dhAvana-mArge prAtaH
SaDvAdanAt sArdhaikAdashavAdanaparyantam parivahanAnAm gamanAgamanaM pidhAsyate|

bhArata-pAka- mahAnideshakaH

bhAratIya-sainikairuktaM yat‌ pAkistAnIya-sainika mukhyAlayasya lakSye
niyantraNa-rekhAyAM ch sainikAnAM kAryeSu bhedo vartate| tena pAkistAnIya-
sainikAH vishvasanIyatAyaH bhedaM dUrIkartuM prasthAnAya proktam|
