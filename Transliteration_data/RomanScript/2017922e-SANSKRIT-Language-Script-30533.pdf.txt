pradhAnamantrI-vArANasI

pradhAnamantrI narendramodI svasya saMsadIya-kSetrasya vArANasI-yAtrAyAM vartate| saH
vArANasyAH dvidivasIya-yAtrAyAm‌ asti| asau vArANasItaH samIpasthe baDA-lAlapura-
grAme dInadayAla-saMkula-nAmnA vyApAra-sauvidhya-kendramudghATitavAn| kendrIya-vastrasya
evaM sUchnA-prasAraNa-mantrI smRti-irAnI, vastra-rAjyamantrI ajaya-TamaTA ch
samArohe'smin‌ samupasthitau AstAm| saH sUrataM vadodarAM vArANasItaH saMyojayat‌
mahAmanA-eksapres relayAnaM dhvaja-purassaram udghATanaM vihitavAn|

bhArata-saMyuktarASTra-pAkistAna

bhAratadeshena pAkistAnaM TeraristAnaH iti udghoSitaH| tatratya bhUmau AtaMkavAdaH
eva utpadyate| nyUyArke saMyukta-rASTra-mahAsabhAyAM bhAratasya sthAyi-mishanasya prathamA
sachivA enama-gaMbhIreNa proktaM yat TeraristAnasya akhila-jagati kA'pi spardhA
nA'sti| tayodIritaM yat‌ jammUkashmIraH bhAratasyAbhinnaH ago'sti|

rASTrapatiH

rASTrapatiH rAmanAtha-kovindaH adya mahArASTrasya nAgapure dIkSA-bhUmau bhArata-
ratnAya bAbA-sAheba-DAM bhImarAva-ambeDakarAya  shraddhAMjali samarpitavAn| asminnavasare
mahArASTrasya rAjyapAlaH sI0 vidyAsAgara-rAvaH, mukhyamantrI devendra phaDaNavIsaH,
kendrIya-mantrI nitina-gaDakarI, vidyut-rAjyamantrI chndrashekhara-bavanakule, meyara-nandA
jichkaraH ch upasthitA Asan| rASTrapatinA kAmpatyAM Degana-pelesa mandire vipAsanA-
dhyAna-kendramudghATitam|

amitashAhaH/vIrabhadraH

bhAratIyajanatApArdItidalAdhyakSeNa amitashAhena himAchlapradeshasya kAgresa-
prashAsanIya sadasyaH vIrabhadrasihaH kaTvAlochitaH| himAchlpradeshe-huMkAra-janasabhAyAM asau
prAbhASata yat kAMgresadalaM pradeshe parivartana-Anayan dishi viphalA jAtA|

jammU-dhRtaH

jammUkashmIre rAmabana janapade banihAla-kSetre sashastra-sIma-balasya dale vihite
ekasmin AkramaNe AtaMkinau dhRtau| eko bhaTo'pi hutAtmabhUto jAtaH|

bhArata-pAkistAna

bharAratapAkistAnayormadhye'dya mahAnideshaka-sainyAbhiyAna-starIya sambhASaNaM
vihitam| vichAraNAvasare tAni sarvANi saMgharSa-virAmollaghIya-prakaraNAni utthitAni
yAni pAkistAnIya-sainyabhaTaireva uttaradAyinaH abhavan| adya ch niyantrarekhAyAM
pAkistAnIya-sahayoginI chmUsenA eva asmadIya sthAnIya-nAgarikANAmupari
golikAH varSati|

iNDoneshiyA

iNDoneshiyAyAH bAlI-dvIpa-samUhe sakriyAgniparvata-kSetrasya pArshve nivasantaH
srahasrAdhika-janAH niSkAsitAH| AdhikArika-sUtraiH nigaditaM yat bAlI-dvIpe-mAuMTa-
aguMga-jvAlAmukheH nikaTataHH SaDa-sahasrAdhika-janAH aparatra preSitAH|

govA

govA vidhAnasabhAyAM vipakSinetA,  kAgresa-dalasya sAMsadashch chndrashekhara
kavalekaraH govArakSidalIya aparAdha-shAkhayA avedhAnika-gatividhiSu saMliStikAraNAt
dhRtaH| AkAshavANItaH saMlApakAle variSTha-govArakSidalasya adhikAriNA proktaM yat
prakaraNe'smin kavalekaraM prati samana-patramekamapi preSitam|

rASTriya-sevA-saMgha-vijayan

variSTha-rASTriya-sevA-saMghasya kAryakartrA je0 nandakumAreNa keralapradeshasya
