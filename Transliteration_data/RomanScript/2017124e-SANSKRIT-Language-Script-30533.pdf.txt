mukhyavArtAH

 gujarAta-vidhAna-sabhA-nirvAchnasya prathama-chraNAya prachArAbhiyAnaM tvarate|

 adya nausenAdivasaH AmAnyate, rASTrapatiH, pradhAnamantrI ch asminnavasare
nausainikebhyaH shubhakAmanAH pradattAH|

 amerikIya-rakSAmantrI jemsa-maiTisaH vAtaurtham‌ pAkistAnaM samprAptavAn‌|

pradhAnamantrI gujarAtaH

pradhAnamantrI narendramodI avAdIt‌ yat gujarAte janAH kAMgresadalaM naiva
svIkariSyanti, yato hi dalamidaM nimnavargavirodhI vartate| dharamapure adya ekasyAM
janasabhAyAM modinA proktaM yat kAMgresadalena anya-nimnavargAyogaH saMvaidhAnikaH naiva
kRtaH| AkAshavANI-vArtAharaH asUchyat‌ yat anyeSAM rAjanItika-dalAnAM
shIrSanetAro'pi antimaparyAye prachArAbhiyAne niratAH santi|

sItAramaNaH

rakSA-mantrI nirmalA-sItAramaNaH abravIt yat chkravAta-prabhAviteSu kSetreSu kendra-
rAjyabalAbhyAM uddhAra-kAryANi satvaraM prachAlyante| rakSAmantrI tiruanantapuram-janapade
vijahinajAne pUnathUrAyAM ch chkravAtena prabhAvitaiH janai saha vArtAM karoti sma|

bhAratIya-nausenA

adya nausenAdivasaH AmAnyate| nausainikAnAm‌ upalabdhi-bhUmikAshch pradarshayituM
disambaramAsasya chturthe dinAMke eSaH divasaH samAyojyate| atrAvasare rASTrapatinA
rAmanAthakovindena pradhAnamantriNA narendramodinA ch nausenAyai shubhakAmanAH vyAhatAH|

vitta-Ayoga-sammelanam‌
paMchdasha-vittAyogasya prathamam‌ upaveshanam‌ adya navadillyAM sampannam|
prashAsanena gatamAse eva AyogaH sthApitaH AsIt| asya adhyakSaH pUrvasachivaH
sAMsadashch ena0 ke0 siMhaH vartate| upaveshane'smin‌ kendrIya-prashAsanasya
rAjyaprashAsanAnAM sthAyi-nikAyAnAM pachAyatAnAM netRbhiH rAjItika-dalaiH mantribhiH saha
vyApaka-vichAra-vimarshasya nirNayaH kRtaH|

pAkistAnaM maiTisaH

amerikIya-rakSAmantrI jemsa-maiTisaH adya dvipakSIya-kSetrIyaviSayeSu ch
pAkistAnasya pradhAnamantriNaM sthalasenAdhyakSaM ch sametya prashAsana-sainya-netRshniH
vArtArtham islAmAbAdaM samprAptavAn| vArtAsu AtaMkasharaNa-sthalInAM viSayaH prAmukhyaM
bhajate|

rASTrapatiH

rASTrapatiH rAmanAthakovindaH pratyapAdayat‌ yat‌ pAshchAtyajIvanashailinA
samutpannarogaiH janAH drutatayA prabhAvitAH bhavanti| asyAM sthitau AyurvedaH
mahattvapUrNa-bhUmikAM nirvoDhuM shaknoti| adya rASTrapatibhavane paNDita- rAmanArAyaNa-
sharmA-rASTriya-puraskAra-pradAnAvadhau shrIkovindaH uktavAn yat vishvaM bhAratAt roga-
nivAraNasya vaikalpika-praNAlIM kAmayate|

uparASTrapatiH

uparASTrapatinA veMkaiyA-nAyaDunA adya kAnapuranagare chndrashekhara-AjAda-kRSi-
vishvavidyAlayasya unaviMshati-dIkSAnta-samArohe mukhyAtithitvena chAtrAH samAhUtAH yat
te kRSisambaddha-vividhaviSayeSu shodhaM kRtvA abhinavopAyaiH kRSivikAsaM sAdhayeyuH|
shrInAiDa़uH parAmarshitavAn‌ yat‌ kRSi-shikSA vidyAlaya-pAThyakrame saMyojanIyA|

rAhula-nAmAMkanam‌

kAMgresopAdhyakSaH rAhula-gAMdhI adya navadil‍lyAM dalasya mukhyAlaye dalAdhyakSa-
padAya nAmAMkanaM kRtavAn| nAmAMkanasya adya antima-tithiH AsIt| dalasya netA
kamalanAthaH, shrImatI shIlA-dIkSitaH, shrI motIlAlavoharA taruNa-gogoI ch adhyakSapadAya
rAhula-gAMdhinaH prastAvakAH santi|

sharIpha-uchch-nyAyAlayaH

pAkistAne islAmabAdochchnyAyAlayena apadastha-pradhAnamantriNaH navAja-
sharIphasya prArthanA asvIkRtA yat bhraSTAchArasya prakaraNatrayANAM samameva vAdashravaNaM
kuryAditi| itaH pUrvam‌ islAmAbAdasya uttaradAyi-nyAyAlayenApi eSA prArthanA
gatamAsasya aSTame tithau nirastA AsIt‌|
