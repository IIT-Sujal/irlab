yugapat‌ triHtalAka-prathAyAH samApana-viSayaka vidheyaka prastotuM hyaH
kendrIya-mannriNAM vRndamekaM mantraNopaveshanaM samAyojitavat‌ vRndameka

mantriNaH-triHtalAkam‌

yugapat triHtalAka-prathAyAH samApana-viSayakaM vidheyakaM prastotuM hyaH kendrIya-
mantriNAM vRndamekaM mantraNopaveshanaM samAyojitavat vRndamekaM mantraNopaveshanaM
samAyojitavat| evaM vishvasyate yat kendraM saMsadaH AgAmini shItakAlike satre
vidheyakamidaM sadanapaTale prastotuM vAnJchati| AdhikArika-sUtraiH saMsUchitaM yad gRha-
mantriNo rAjanAthasiMhasya AdhyakSye sampannAyAmetasyAM mantraNAyAm prastAvita-
vidheyakena saMbaddhAH naike pakSAH savistaraM parichrchitAH|

kendrama-rAjyAni-chikitsAlayAH



kendraM rAjyAni niradishat‌ yad‌ deshe avaidhAnikeSu anaitikeSu ch
vyApArAchraNeSu saMliptAn‌ chikitsAlayAn‌ virudhya prabhAvi-padakSetra gRhItvA taiH
svasvarAjye upachAragRhasthApanAdhiniyamaH sadyaH pravartanIyaH| hariyANAyAH gurugrAma-
janapada-sthena phorTisa-chikitsAlayena vigata-dineSu kasmAchchid‌ rugNajanAn‌
atishulkAdAna-prakaraNaM saMdRbhNatA svAsthya-mantrAlayena samudIritaM yadetAdRshAH
naijAH api krUra-chikitsAlayAH sadya eva saMchetanIyAH|

prashAsanam-dhanAdeshapatrasauvidhyam

vitta-mantrAlayena spaSTIkRtaM baiMkeSu grAhakebhyaH samupalabdhasya chaikabukaH iti
dhanAdeshapatrAvali-sauvidhyasya parAvartana-viSaye na ko'pi prastAvaH samprati
vichArAdhIno vidyate| vigata-divaseSu kaishchid jana-saMchAra-mAdhyamaiH eSa pravAdaH
prasAritaH yat kendrashAsanaM deshe aMkapravidhi-yutAni baiMkIya-AdAna-pradAnAni
protsAhayituM shIghameva chekabukaH iti dhanAdesha-patrANi parAvartayitumarhati|

 

ja़imbAbve-nUtanarASTrapatiH

jimbAbve-deshIyaH pUrvopa-rASTrapatiH imarsana-manAGgAgvA adya taddeshasya
nUtana-rASTrapatitvena shapathaM grAhayate| shapatha-samArohaH jimbAbve-rAjadhAnyAM harAre-
nagaryAmavasthite SaSTi-sahasra-janopaveshana-kSamatA-yute sabhAgAre samAyojiSyate|
atredaM dhyAnAspadaM vidyate yat tatra deshe-rASTrapati-padaM vigata-divaseSu rAbarTa-mugAbe-
dvArA padatyAga-kAraNAt‌ riktamajAyata|

videshamantrAlayaH-dvipAkSikamantraNam‌

videshamantrI suSamA-svarAjaH adya navadillyAM phinalaiNDa-deshIya-
videshamantriNaM TImo-soinIM saMmilati| ubhau netArau vyApAra-vinivesha-prabhRtiSu
pramukha-kSetreSu deshadvasya pArasparikAn sahayoga-sambandhAn vivardhayituM vistRta-
chrchAmAchriSyataH|
