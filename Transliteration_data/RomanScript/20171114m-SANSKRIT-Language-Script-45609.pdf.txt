jeTalI-vastusevAkaraH

vittamaMtrI aruNa-jeTalI kAMgresa--dalasya abhighoSaH nirAkRtavAn‌ yat‌ bhUyo'pi dvishataM
vastUnAM karaM nyUnIkartuM kAMgresa-dalena prashAsanaM vivashIkRtam‌| tenodIritaM yat‌ karamitInAM
yuktatA-vaSaye triSu chrtuSu mAseSu kAryaM vidhIyate sma, nairvAchnikena uta vA
rAjanItikAdhyarthanena saha asya saMyojanaM bAlarAjanIti iva pratIyate|

samAnakara-pravartanArthaM kAMgresa-upAdhyakSasya adhyarthanA nirAkurvatA tena proktaM yat‌ samprati

karamitInAM saralIkaraNaM vidhAsyate|
