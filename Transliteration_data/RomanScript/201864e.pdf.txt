rASTrapatiH

rASTrapatinA rAmanAtha koviMdenoktaM yat rAjyapAlAH vikAsa-
yojanebhyaH vaMchita-janAnAM kalyANAya mahatvapUrNa-bhUmikAH nivoDhuM shakyante|
shrIkovidaH adya rASTrapati-bhavane rAjyapAla-upa-rAjyapAlAnAm Una-

saptatiH pratishat‌ vishvavidyAlayAH rAjya-prashAsanAntarma samAyAnti, tatra
rAjyapAlAH eva kulAdhipatayaH vartante|

bhavitavyA| tena bhaNitaM yat kendraM varSe'smin aktUbara-dvitIyAtaH
chturviMshati-mAsa-paryantaM mahAtmA-gAndhinaH panJchAshaduttara-shata-tamI jayantIm‌
paripAlayiSyati| sammelane uparASTrapatiH ema. vaikaiyA nAyaDa़H, pradhAnamantrI

prArambhika-satraM sambodhitam‌| tena bhaNitaM yat‌ ekaviMshe divase antArASTiya-
