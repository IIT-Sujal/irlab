Arthika-sarvekSaNam‌

iNDiyA-iMka iti ArthikasarvekSaNasamabaddhasamAvAyena deshasya Arthikasthitau pariSkArAya
mAnavasampadA-kRSi-utpAdanakSetrayoH abhyunnatiH sabalaM pratipAditA| aiSamaH vikAsa-mitiH
pAdona-sapta pratishataM bhavet| Arthika-sarvekSaNe proktaM yat AgAmi-varSe deshasya sakala-gRhyotpAdane
saptataH sArddha-sapta-pratishanmitA vRddhiH bhaviSyati| vittamantriNA arUNa jeTalinA gatadine saMsadi
ArthikasarvekSaNaM prastutam | sarvekSaNe prabalaM pratipAditaM yat vastu-sevA-karAt arthavyavasthAyAM
tIvratA samAyAti|

ArthikasarvekSaNam‌

deshe panJchAyatastareSu karasaMgrahaNa-staraM svalpam‌ avartata| saGghIyarAjakoSAya
karasaMgrahakartRbhyashch viSayo'yaM samAhvAnaM vartate| gatadine saMsadi vigatavarSasya ArthikasarvekSaNam‌
upasthApayatA vittamantriNA aruNajeTalinA pratipAditaM yat‌ rAjyaiH panJchnavati mitaM rAjasvaM
kendrAt‌ rAjyashAsanAt‌ vA prApyate| taiH svIyasaMsAdhanaiH pratishataM panJchmitaM dhanasaGgrahaNaM vidhIyate|

pashchimabaGgAla

pashchimabaGgAlasya murshidAbAda-janapade rAjakIyaparivahanavibhAgasya basayAnasya dhogarA
kUlyAM nimajjanAt SaDatriMshat janAH mRtAH| mRtakeSu dashamahilAH api santi| rAjyasya mukhyamantrI
mamatAbanarjI mRtakANAm AshritebhyaH panJch lakSarUpakANAM vraNitebhyashch ekalakSyarUpyakANAM
sAhAyyarAshiH udaghoSitavatI|

bhArata-viyatanAma-yuddhAbhyAsaH

bhArata-viyatanAmadeshayoH sainyabalaiH gatadine madhyapradeshasya jabalapure SaD divasIyo
yuddhAbhyAsaH prArabdhaH| yuddhAbhyAsaH deshadvayasya sAmarika-rakSAsambandhAnAM dRDhIkaraNaM prastauti|
vinabaiksa nAmakaH bhArataviyatanAmayoH prathamo'yaM yuddhAbhyAsaH vartate|

pUvottiravimAnapattanam‌

bhAratIya-vimAnapattanaprAdhikaraNena pUrvottarakSetrasya vimAnapattanAnAM staronnayanA
chtutriMshat‌ koTirupyANAM rAshiH AbaNTitaH| prAdhikaraNasya adhyakSaH guruprasAdamahApAtrA gauhAyyAM
vArtAharaH saha sambhASaNAvadhau prAvocht‌ yat‌ chtuH chtvAriMshaduttaranavashatakoTirupyakANAM
pariyojanAH pUrvameva pUrNAH avabhavan| sheSakAryaM varSadvaye varSatraye vA pUrNaM bhaviSyati|
