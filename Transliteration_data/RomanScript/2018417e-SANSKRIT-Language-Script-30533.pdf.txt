pradhAnamantrI-sTokahoma

pradhAnamantrI narendramodI adyAparAhaNe svIDana-pradhAnamantriNA “sTIphana
lUphavena” ityamunA samaM mahattvapUrNa dvaipAkSikaM sambhASaNaM vyadadhAt| ubhAbhyAmapi
netRbhyAM mahattvapUrNeSu dvaipAkSika-kSetrIya-prakaraNeSu rachnAtmaka-mantraNA vihitA|
mantraNAnantaram‌ imau yugapadeva sogara-hAusa-taH rojanabaiDa-paryantaM padayAtrAmapi
kRtavantau| shrImodI svIDana-rAjnJA kArla SoDasha gustApha' ityanena samamapi
sambhASitavAn‌|

vittamantrI

vittamantriNA aruNa-jeTalinA nigadyate yat‌ ArASTre paryApta-dhanapatrANi
upalabdhAni santi| anenauktaM yad‌ AvashyakatAM prapUrayitu panJchshataM, dvishataM, shataM
cheti mudrApatrANi paryAptamAtrAyAm upalabhyante| shrI jeTalinA deshasya katipaya
kSetreSu mudrApatrANAmabhAvasya sUchnAnantaraM mudrAsthitiH samIkSitA asti| asau
nyagadat yad e.TI.ema. yantreSu dhanasya paryAptApUrti sunishchetuM prashAsanaM rijarva
baiMkaM ch sarvasambhavopAyAn vidadhataH|

vANijyodyogamantrI-aMkIkaraNam

vANijyodyogamantriNA sureshaprabhuNA sUkSma-laghu-madhyama-udyamAnAM
niryAtakebhyaH aMkIkaraNa-sauvidhyaM sulabhAyituM “DijiTala epha. AI. I. o.
globala liMkara” ityasya shubhArambho vihitaH| shrIprabhuNA navadillyAm‌
vebaphalakamidam udghATayatA proditaM yat asmin‌ phalake pradhAnamantriNo
parikalpanAyAH naike AyAmAH samAveshitAH santi| amunA niryAta-kSetre kAryaratAH
same'pi vaNijaH sva-sahayogibhiH asmin vebaphalake nAmAMkanapUrvakaM vaishvikApaNeSu
bhAratasya vyApakopasthitiM pradarshayitum AhUtAH|

khAdyamantrI rAmavilAsa-pAsavAnaH nyagadat yat rASTriya-prajAtAntrika-saMyuteH
prashAsanaM dalitAnAM hitaiSi vartate| navadillyAM patrakAraiH samaM sambhASaNAvasare asau
nyagadat yat asheSe'pi deshe dalitotthAnAya dalita-sashaktIkaraNAya ch bhUri
upAyAH kriyante|

karNATaka-vidhAnasabhAnirvAchnAni

karNATaka-vidhAnasabhA nirvAchnArthamadya adhisUchnAyAH prakhyApanena sahaiva
nAmAMkana-patra-prapUraNasya prakriyA samArabdhA| nAmAMkanapatrANi apraila chturviMshatiM
yAvat prapUrayituM shakSyante| etAni apraila-panJchviMshyAM nirIkSayiSyante| apraila-
saptaviMshati-paryantaM nAmAMkanaM pratyAvartituM shakSyate| asmin nirvAchna-samare
bhAjapAdalena chtuH panJchAshaduttaraikashataM kA~gresadalena aSTAdashottara-dvishataM, janatAdala
saikyulara iti dalena SaDa-viMshati-adhika-ekashataM pratyAshinAM nAmAni ghoSitAni
santi| chturvishatyadhika-dvi-shata-sadasyAtmikAyai vidhAnasabhAyai maI-dvAdashyA
matadAnaM bhaviSyati|

aTala nivRtti-vetana-yojanA

aTala-nivRtti-vetana-yojanAyAM prAshAsanika-yogadAnarUpeNa adhunAvadhi prAyaH
ekaviMshati-koTyadhika-ekaarbuda-rUpyakANi prakhyApitAni santi| sarvakArIya-
vijnJpteranusAreNa apraila-dvAdvarshIM yAvat asyAH yojanAyAH antargataM SaSTi-
lakSAdhika-sapta-navati-koTito'pi-adhikAH aMshadAtAraH panJchIkRtAH Asan| aTala-
nivRtti-vetana-yojanAyAM SaSTi-varSa-vayaH prabhRti aMshadAtre pratimAsaM ekasahasrataH
panJchsahasrarupyakANi pradIyante|

jammU-kashmIra-saMgharSaH

jammU-kashmIre ajnJAta-AtaMkinAM surakSA-bhaTAnAM ch madhye golikAbhiH saMgharSaH
prachlati| eSaH saMgharSaH dakSiNa-kashmIrasya pulavAmA-janapade trAlakSetre sIra grAme
surakSA-prayANa-rateSu surakSAbhaTeSu, AtaMkibhiH sahasA golikA-varSaNena samArabdhaH|
surakSA-trotobhiH AkAshavANI saMsUchitA yat tatra AtaMkinAmAkraNAnantaraM sainya-
ArakSi-kendrIyArakSitArakSi-balAnAM bhaTaiH samastamapi kSetraM paridhIkRtya
anveSaNAbhiyAnaM prachAtitamasti|

saMyukatarASTrama-bhAratam‌
