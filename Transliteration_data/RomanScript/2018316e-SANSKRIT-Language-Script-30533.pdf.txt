pradhAnamaMtrI

pradhAnamaMtrI narendramodI imphAlanagarasthe maNipura-vishvavidyAlaye adya bhAratIya-
vijnJAna-kAMgresam udghATayat| tena vaijnJAnikAH samAhUtAH yat prayogashAlAsu shodhakAryANi
kRSakANAM lAbhAya kalpanIyAni| shrImodI avAdIt yat anusandhAnena deshasya unnatiH
vikAso ch bhavet| pradhAnamantrI avAdIt yat vijnJAna-praudyogikyo prayoge bhAratasya
samRddha-paraMparA vartate| anena sAmAnya-janAnAM lAbho bhavet|

saMsada

vibhinneSu viSayeSu vipakSidalAnAM kolAhalavashAt saMsadaH sadanayoH kAryajAtaM
adyApi bAdhitam| lokasabhAyAH kAryajAtaM prathamaM madhyAhaNaM yAvat sthagitam| kAryajAtaM
yadA punaH samArabdhaH tadA e.AI-e.DI.ema.ke. dalIya sadasyAH sadanamadhye samAgatAH|
kintu sadane abyavasthAM dRSTvA adhyakSayA sadanaM somavAsaraM yAvat sthagitam |

rAjyasabhA tu prathamaM sArdha-dvi-vAdanaM yAvat sthagitam| yadA punaH sadanasya kArya
samArabdhaM sabhApatinA telagudesham pArTI iti dalasya vAI-esa.chaudharI svabhASaNam
anuvartayitum AhUtaH| paraM sadane avyavasthA eva AsIt tadA sabhApatinA sadana
somavAsaraM yAvat sthagitam|
