pradhAnamantrI

pradhAnamantriNA narendramodinA abhihitaM yat‌ hiMsAyAH AtaMkavAdasya
ch nirAkaraNaM rikthasya mUlyAnAM balena sambhavet‌| navadillyAm‌ islAmika
rikthaM udAravAdasya saMvardhanam cheti viSaye Ayojite kAryakrame shrImodinA
uktaM yat na ko'pi dharmaH hiMsAchraNaM shikSayati| niraparAdhinaH virUddhaya
jaghanya-hiMsAchraNe saMliptAH kasyApi dharmasya anuyAyinaH bhavituM nArhanti|
tena bhaNitaM yat‌ yuvabhiH islAmasya mAnavIyamUlyaiH anuprANitAH bhavantu|
pragataye sarvaiH vijnJAnasyopayogaH kartavyaH|
