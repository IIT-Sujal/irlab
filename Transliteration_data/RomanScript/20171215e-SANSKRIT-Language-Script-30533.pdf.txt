rAjyasabhA-sthaganam‌

sthagitamabhUt| vipakSinetA gulAmanavI AjAdaH, samAjavAdI pArTIti-dala-sadasyaH naresha-agravAlashch
prakaraNamidaM sadane utthApayitukAmau AstAm kintu sabhApatinA ema.veMkaiyA nAyaDunA anumatiH naiva pradattA|
punaH sadanasya kAryajAtAnAm Arabdhe sati AjAdena gujarAtanirvAchna prachArAvasare pradhAnamantridvArA
pUrvapradhAnamantriNaH manamohanasiMhasya anyeSAM ch pAkistAnIya-shiSTamaNDalena saha upaveshanAchraNasya Aropa

sarvochchnyAyAlayaH H AdhArapatram‌

sarvochch nyAyAlayena vibhinna-prAshAsanika-sevA, kalyANakara-yojanAnAM ch lAbha-svIkaraNArtham‌

rASTrapatiH

bAbA sAheba-bhImarAva-ambeDakara-vishvavidyAlayasya saptame dIkSAnta samArohe janAn sambodhayati smA

yadAbhyAM netubhyAM rASTrasya AdhAraH sudRDha़IkRtAH | rASTrapatinA kovindena tatra pratibhAvadbhyaH chAtrebhyaH
padakAnyapi vitaritAni|
