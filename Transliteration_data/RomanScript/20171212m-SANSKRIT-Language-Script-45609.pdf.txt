bhAratIyArthavyavasthA-saMyuktarASTram

bhAratIyA artha-vyavasthA AgAmini varSe sapta-dashamalava-dvi-mitAM,
tadanuvartini varSe ch sapta-dashamalava-chtuHparimitAM ch vRddhimupayAsyati|
etat pUrvAkalanaM ch saMyukta-rASTra-pariSadaH Arthika-sAmAjika-viSaya-

yajjagataH ashIti-koTyadhikAH janAH adyatve bhojyAnnAnAm abhAvAt
prANa-sandhAraNArthaM saMgharSa-ratAH vidyante| etachch vaktavyaM gatadivase
