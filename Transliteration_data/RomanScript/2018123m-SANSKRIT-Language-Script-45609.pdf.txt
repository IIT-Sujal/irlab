adhyakSayiSyate| sammelane'smin shrImodI jagataH shreSThaiH udyoga-patibhniH
bhAratIya-kArya-kAryadhikAribhishchApi sArdha mantrayiSyate| pradhAnamantriNA
tatropasthitasya vishva-vANijyodyoga-pati-vRndasya samakSaM nUtana-bhAratasya

antAraSTariyamudrAkoSaH-bhAratam‌

antArASTriya-mudrA-koSeNa prochyate yad bhAratam pravartamAne aSTAdhika-
dvi-sahasra-tame varSe prativeshinashchInadeshasya SaD-dashamalava-aSTa-mita-
vRddhyAkalanApekSayA pratishataM sapta-dashamalava-chturmitAm Arthika-
vRddhimavApya jagatastIvratamA arthavyavasthA seddhumarhati| etachch tathyaM
gatadivase dAvose prasArite vaishvike ArthikAkalane prakAshamAnItam‌|

bhAratam‌-vishvabaiMkam‌
