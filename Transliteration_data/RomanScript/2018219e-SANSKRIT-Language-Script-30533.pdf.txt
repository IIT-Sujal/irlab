sIbIAI-pIenabI

sIbIAI iti vibhAgena adya pajAba-naishanala-baikasya mumbaIsthA
braiDIsadanashAkhA avaruddhA, pravaMchnAprakaraNe mukhyAropiNaH niravamodinaH tasya
sahayoginashch sambandho anayA shAkhayA AsIt| aparatra pravartanideshAlayenApi
prakaraNe'smin puNe-thANe-mumbaInagareSu sahasAnvIkSaNAbhiyAnaM saMchAlitam|

sIbIAI-roTomaika

kendrIyAnveSaNavibhAgena roTomaika-pena iti samavAyapramukhaM vikramakoThArIm‌
anyAshch virUddhaya prAthamikI paMjIkAritA asti| ete vibhinnabaikebhyaH adhigata-
aSTArbud‌-rAsheH RNapratyAvartane doSiNo santi| atrAntare saubauAI dalena kAnapurastha-
koThArI ityasya anyeSAM parisampattiH anvIkSate|

prashAsanam-rijarvabaiMkam

rijarva-baiMka sarveSAM baiMkAnAM viniyAmako vartate| prashAsanena paMjAba-neshanala-baiMka
pravaMchnA-prakaraNe tasya tattvAvadhAne pramAda-viSaye patraM likhitamasti| vittamantrAlaye
uchch-starIya-strotAMsi kathayanti yat prashAsanena rijarva baiMka kathitaM yat tat baiMkeSu
prAvaMchnAni udghATayituM niyamana-vyavasthAyAH sAmarthyaM parIkSeta| atrAntare
prakaraNamidamAlakSya adya navadillyAM kendrIya-satarkatA-Ayoga-rijarvabaiMka-paMjAba-
neshanala-baiMkAdhikAriNAm upaveshanaM saMjAtam|
