haidrAbAda-makkA

visphoTe bhUmikAyAH pramANane asamarthamasti| saptottara-dvi-sahasratame varSe haidrAbAde makkA-masjideprArthanAvadhau

bhAjapA-dalenoktaM yat etena nirNayena kAMgresadalasya tuSTIkaraNa-rAjanItiH atha dharma-visheSe mithyA-
