pradhAnamantrI narendramodI chIna-rASTrapatiH shI chinapiMgaH iti netRdvayormadhye bhArata-chIna-
sImAntakSetreSu shAnti-saMyama-sandhAraNAya parichrchA abhUt| chInadeshasya vuhAnanagare netRdrayoH

rASTrapatiH

rASTranirmANe sahAyakaM syAt|
