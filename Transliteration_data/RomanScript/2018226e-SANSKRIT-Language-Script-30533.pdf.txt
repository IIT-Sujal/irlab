uparASTrapatiH

uparASTrapatiH ema.vekaiyAnAyaDuH udIritavAn yat paMjAba-neshanalabaike
pravaMchnA-prakaraNe prabaMdhanasya viphalatA parilakSyate| adya nava-dillyAM
pradhAnamantrI shrama-puraskArANAM pradAnAnantaraM tena bhaNitaM yat prabandhanaM
samyak‌ bhavatu yena bhaviSye etAdRshyAH ghAnAH na bhaveyuH| shrInAyaGunA
aSa triMshaduttara-trishata-shramikebhyaH ekAdashottara-dvisahasravarSAd‌ Arabhya SoDashaM
yAvat‌ puraskArAH pradattAH| tenetadapi proktaM yat‌ shramikANAM shaikSika-stara
samunnetuM guNavattAyutaM prashikSaNam Avashyakam|

kendrIyAnveSaNa-vibhAgena nijI-sharkarA-samavAyeNa siMbhAvalI shugara
limiDa dvArA kapaTAchraNa-prakaraNe trayodashajanAn‌ virUdadhaya prAthamikI
pajikAritAsti| eteSu paMjAbasya mukhyamantriNaH amaridara-siMhasya jAmAtA
samavAyasya upaprabandhaka nideshakaH gurapAla siMhaH, adhyakSaH guramIta-siMha-
mAn ch vartete| orieMTala baiMka ऑpha kaॉmarsa ityanena ekAdashottara-

koTirUpyakANA RNaM pradatta paraM kapaTarItyA taddhana svIyAvashyakatAnAM pUrtaye
anyatra vyayIkRtam‌, yena baikasya navauttaraikashata-ko -rUpyakANAM hAniH
abhavat‌|
