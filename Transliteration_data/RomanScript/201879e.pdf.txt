pradhAnamantrI

pradhAnamaMtrI narendramodI, dakSiNakoriyAyAH rASTrapatiH mUna je ina uttarapradeshe noeDAyAM adya sAyam saimasaMga
nirmANashAlAM gamiSyataH| tatra tau vishvasya bRhattama-jaMgamadUrabhASa utpAdanakendram uddhATayiSyataH| etasmAt deshe meka ina
iMDiyA-kaushala vikAsa yojanayoH dIrgha-balamAyAsyati| navadillyAM chturdivasIya yAtrAprasaMge dakSiNa-koriyA-rASTrapatinA
bhArata-koriyA-vyApAra-maMche api sahabhAgitvaM kRtam‌| tena gAndhI smRti-sthalasyApi shrImodinA sAkaM yAtrA vihitA|

sureshaprabhaH

vANijyodyoga mantriNA sureshaprabhunA-dakSiNa-koriyAyAH vyApAra-mantriNA kima-hmunachoMga-varyeNa pArasparika-
vyApAraM vivardhituM sandhipatrANi navadillyAmadya hastAkSaritAni| vArtAharaissaha samvadan shrIprabhuH udIritavAn yat deshayoH
sambandhAn‌ agresaraNe vyApAra-vANijyasya mahattvapUrNa-bhUmikA vidyate| dakSiNa-koriyAyAH vyApAra-mantriNA kima-hyana chAga
varyaNoktaM yat‌ pradhAnamantriNaH  narendramodinaH  meka-ina-iMDiyA-nItyAntargataM prathama-prayAso'yaM vidyate| shrIchogaH pradhAnamantriNA
modinA sAkaM saimasaMga-utpAdana-samavAyaM daSTaM gamiSyati, samavAye asmin‌ dvAviMshatyuttara-dvi-sahasnatama varSaparyantaM
koTyadhika-jaMgama dUrabhASa yantrANAM vArSika-kSamatA bhavitA|

uttarapradeshaH

uttarapradeshe kukhyAta-aparAdhinaH premaprakAsha-siMhasya urpha munnA bajaraMginaH adya prAtaH bAgapata-kArAgAre hatyA-
prakaraNe chtvAraH adhikAriNaH udayapratApasiMhaH, shivAjIyAdavaH, arijindara-siMhaH, mAdhavakumArashch nilambitAH| meraTha-
kSetrasya atirikta--ArakSi-mahAnideshakena prashAMta-kumAreNa AkAshavANI sUchitA yat kArAgAre dalayormadhye pArasparika-
pratidvandatAyAM munnAbajaraMgI nihataH| asau anekeSu AparAdhika-prakareSu, hatyAsu, apaharaNeSu saMliptaH AsIt‌|
amaranAthayAtrA

jammUkashmIre vArSikI amaranAthayAtrA pahalagAma-bAlatala mAgAt‌ gahana-surakSA-prabandheSu suchArUtayA agresarati|
yatreyaM hyaH kashmIropatyakAyAM avarodhanAnantaraM punaH prArabdhA| vAtAvaraNa-anukUlanena bAlatala-mArgAt‌ yAtrA prArabdhAsti|
dvimAsAtmikI-pavitra-amaranAtha-yAtreyaM agasta SaDviMshe shrAvaNa-pUrNimA-utsave avasAnaM prati yAsyati|



jammUkashmIre'dya ajnJAtAtaMkibhiH kendayArAkSitA-rakSibale hasta-golakaiH AkramaNaM kRtam‌| baraguNDa grAme tAla
kSetre etena AkramaNena kSateH sUchnA nAdhigatAsti| atrAntare ekasya yUnasya shavaH dakSiNa-kashmIre shopiyAM grAme adhigataH|
ArakSidalena prakaraNamidaM paMjIkRtvA anvIkSaNAbhiyAnaM prachAlitam‌|

AIAITI dil‍lI

mAnavasaMsAdhana vikAsa mantrAlayena AIAITI dil‍lI, AIAITI bambaI, AIAIesasI baMgalaura-pramukha-
saMsthAnebhyaH sthAnaM pradattam‌| TUvITa-sandeshe virNayo'yaM shrAvayan‌ mAnava-saMsAdhana-vikAsa-mantrI prakAshajAvaDekaraH proktavAn‌
yat nirNayo'yaM aitihAsikaM vartate, etAni saMsthAnAni pUrNa-svAyattIpUrNAni vidyante| tena bhaNitaM yat etasmAt etAni
pramukhANi saMthAnAni drutagatyA vikAsaM kariSyanti| nijI kSetre maNipAla uchch akAdamI bIAITIesa pilAnI, jiyo
saMsthAnAni ch pramukhANi saMsthAnAni udghoSitAH|
meghAlaya

divasIyopaveshanasya gRhamantrI rAjanAthasiMhaH adhyakSatAM vidhAsyati| asmadIya vArtAhareNa vijnJApitaM yatU kendra-prashAsana-
dvArA pUrvottara-pariSadaM gRha-mantrAlayasya niyantraNAdhInAnantaraM idaM prathamamupaveshanaM vidyate|
tamilanADu

mumbaI-varSA
