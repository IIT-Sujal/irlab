dillI pradUSaNam‌

yat‌ adhikAMsha-sthalareSu janaiH adya samuchitAnubhUtiH vihitA|

gujarAtavidhAnasabhA nirvAchnaprachAraH

nirvAchnAyogaH

mukhyanirvAchnAdhikAribhiH saha nirvAchnAt‌ prAk‌ sajjatA-viSayakopaveshanaM kariSyati|

vArtAbhikaraNaM proktavAn‌ yat‌ nirvAchnAyogaH rAjyeSu (vIvIpIeTI) iti matadAtA-
satyApana-patra-yantrANAM prayogasya samIkSA kariSyati|

chitrakUTa upanirvAchnam‌

prasAraNadivasaH

adya lokasevA-prasAraNa-divaso'sti| gata-shatAbdyAH sapta-chtvAriMshattame varSe
