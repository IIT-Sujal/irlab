mukhyavArtAH


 viMshatiH varSANi anantaraM bhArottolane bhAratasya mIrAbAI chAnU punaH svarNapadakaM vijitavatI, rASTrapatiH tAM
maNipurarAjyaM ch hArdika-shubhechchAM pradattavAn|

 pradhAnamaMtrI narendramodI avAdIt yat bhraSTAchAramuktavikAsa praNAlI prashAsanasya prAthamikatA vartate|

jammUkashmIre baDgAma-bArAmUlA janapadayoH surakSAbalaiH saha saMgharSe paMch AtaMkinaH vyApAditAH|

mIrAbAI

mIrAbAI chAnu vishva-bhArottolana-spardhAyAM svarNapadakaM vijitavatI| viMshatiH varSANi anantaraM bhAratena
bhArottolane punaH svarNapadakaM labdhamasti| tayA svavijayena bhArottolane navInA rASTrIyaM kIrtimAnaM sthApitam|
amarIkAyAH enAhema-nagare AyojyamANAyAM spardhAyAM mAdakapadArthasevanArope rUsa-chIna-kajAksthAna-ukrena-
ajarabaijAnAdi -deshAnAM bhArottolakAH bhAgaM grahItum nAnumatAH|

rASTrapatiH rAmanAthakoviMdaH mIrAbAI chAnUM maNipurarAjyaM prati ch hArdika-shubhechchAM pradattavAn|

 

narendramodI

pradhAnamaMtrI narendramodI avAdIt yat bhraSTAchAra-mukta-vikAsa-praNAlI prashAsanasya prAthamikatA vartate|
adya navadillyAM hindustAna-TAimsa-lIDarashipa-sammelane shrImodI uktavAn‌ yat‌ chturdashAdhika dvisahasratame varSe
janAH vyavasthAyAM parivartanArthaM matadAnaM kRtavantaH| evaM deshasya vikAsAya dRDhAdhArasya sthApanA abhavat‌|
shrImodI uktavAn‌ yat‌ prashAsanasya kRte bhraSTAchAramukta-nAgarikonmukha-vikAsasahAyakavyavasthAyAH
prathamikatA vartate|


jammUkashmIrasya bArAmUlA-baDa़gAma-janapadeSu surakSAbalaiH saha saMgharSa paMch AtaMkinaH vyApAditAH|
madhyakashmIrasya baDa़gAma janapade phuTaliporA grAme adya prAtaH saMjAte saMgharSe chtvAraH AtaMkinaH mRtAH|
uttarakashmIre bArAmUlAjanapade soporasya sagIporA-vipine anyasmin‌ saMgharSaM paMchmaH AtaMkI mRtaH|

jeTalI

vittamaMtrI arUNajeTalI avAdIt‌ yat‌ dashakadvaye AdhArabhUta saMrachnAyAH drutanirmANinaiva bhArataM
vikasitaarthavyavasthA bhavet| adya navadillyAM rakSA-sampadA-samArohe shrIjeTalI uktavAn yat AdhArabhUta
saMrachnAyAH nirmANaM shUnye na bhavet| tenoktaM yat rAjasvasyApradAnam deshasya vikAse mukhyA bAdhA asti|
upabhoktubhiH bhadratarasaMrachnAsauvidhyArthaM mUlyaM pradAtavyam‌| shrIjeTalI proktavAn‌ yat‌ sarvakAreNa rAjasyavyavasthAyAH
suchArUsaMchAlanArthaM sarvavidhaprayAsAH vidhIyante|

prasAra-bhAratI

janahita prasArakasya prasArabhAratI-nigamasya mukhya-kAryakArI-adhikAriNA uktamasti yat sarveSAM
vArtANAM epha.ema.kendrebhyaH ahorAtraM prasAraNaM vichAryate| shashi-shekhara-vempati adya haidarAbAde AkAshavANI-
kendrasya prathame yAtrAvasare abhibhASate sma|

aphagAnistAna

aphagAnistAnasya mukhya-kAryakArI abdullA abdullA avAdIt‌ yat‌ tasya deshaH bhArataM na
kevalaM buhad-arthavyavasthAyAH kAraNAt apitu vishve tasya mahatIbhUmikArthaM sammAnayati| tenoktaM yat deshayo
gaNatAntrikavyavasthAyAM mAnavAdhikAre mAnavasammAne ch dRDhAsthA vartate| asau AtaMkavAda-prabhAvitAn sarvAn
deshAn sUchnA-sahayogAdidvArA AtaMkavAdaM niroddhum AhUtavAn|

bAlI-videsha yAtrI

bAlI dvIpAt dinatrayaM yAvat vimAnapattanasya pidhAnAnantaram adya sahasrasaMkhyAkaiH paryaTakaiH vimAnaiH
palAyanaM kriyate| shabdAyamAnaH jvAlAmukhaH aguGga-parvataH sampratyapi sarvAdhakApadA-stare vartate| vAtasya
gatiparivartanAt tataH nirgatAH bhasmAdhUmAdayaH vimAnapattanaM prati adya na vahanti|
