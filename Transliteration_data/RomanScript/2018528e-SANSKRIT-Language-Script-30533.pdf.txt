mukhyavArtAH

 pradhAnamantriNA narendra modinA proktaM yat ujvalA yojanAyAH sAmAjika-
sashaktikaraNe mahatI bhUmikA vartate, gata-varSeSu dasha-koTi-mahAnasIya vAtyendhana
samparkAH pradattAH|

dasha rAjyeSu chtvAraH lokasabhA-dasha-vidhAnasabhA-AsanAya matadAnaM prachlati|

jammU-kashmIre shopiyAM janapade AtaMki-AkramaNe trayaH sainya bhaTAH AhatAH, pulavAmA
janapade ch saMgharSe vIraH vIragatiM prAptavAn, chAlako ekaH mRto jAtaH|

pradhAnamantrI

pradhAnamantriNA narendra modinA proktaM yat “pradhAnamantrI ujvalA yojanA” taH nirdhana-
vanJchita-dalita-janajAtIya-janAnAM jIvana-staraM bhadrataraM jAyate| eSA yojanA sAmAjika-
sashaktikaraNe'pi kendriya-bhUmikAM nirvAhayati| ujvalA-yojanA lAbhAnvitaiH janaissaha
pradhAnamantriNA 'namo-eppa' mAdhyamena sambhASaNamapi kRtam| etAyAH yojanAyAH lAbhAnvitaiH
samvadanAvasare oDishA-chattIsagaDha़-jammUkashmIra-etAdRshebhyaH pradeshebhyaH mahilAbhissAkaM
pradhAnamantriNA saMlApaM vihitam‌|

nirvAchnam‌

dasha rAjyeSu lokasabhAyAH chturNAM vidhAnasabheya dasha-AsanAnAM kRte upa-nirvAchnAya
adya matadAnaM bhavati| uttara-pradeshe kairAnA-mahArASTre-pAlaghara, bhaMDArA-goMdiyA, nAgAlaiMDe
lokasabhAyAH eka-AsanAya matadAnaM bhavati| etadatirichya uttara-pradesha-panJchAba-bihAra-
jhArakhaNDa-kerala-mahArASTra-meghAlaya, uttarAkhaNDa, pashchima-baMgAlasya ch bahuSu kSetreSu matadAnaM
bhavati| matagaNanA ch guruvAsare bhavitA|

uparASTrapatiH

uparASTrapatinA ema. veMkaiyA nAyaDunA bhaNitaM yat bhArateshaH pAkistAnaM sametya svIya
sarvaiH prativeshi-deshaissaha bhadra-sambandhAH samIhate| jammUnagare adya sI.AI.esa.Ara. ityasya
bhAratIya-samaveta-oSadha-saMsthAna-AI.AI.AI.ema.-ityatra samAyojite samArohe upa-
rASTrapatiH proktavAn yat bhAratadeshaH pAkistAnena sAkamapi vArtAya ichchuko vartate, paraM etadarthaM
pAkistAnaM AtaMki-gatividhayaH avaruddhAH kartavyAH| tena vaijnJAnikaiH janAnAM samAjika-
Arthika-sthiti-bhadratarI kartuM prAkRtika-saMsAdhanaiH lAbhAya samAhvAnaM kRtam|

jammU-kashmIraH

jamma-kashmIre dakSiNa-kashmIrasya shopiyAM janapade turkavAMgana-kSetre prAtaH senAyAH
ekasmin praharaNa-dale AtaMkibhiH AkramaNe adhikAriNaM sametya trayaH surakSAkArmikAH AhatAH|
itaH prAk pulavAmA janapade kAkAporA-kSetre AtaMki-sainyajanayormadhye sanJjAte saMgharSe vIrajano
ekaH vIragatiM prAptavAn, eko sthAnIyo'pi mRtaH rakSA-pravaktrA proktaM yat AkramaNe ekasya
vIrasya mRtyupuSTiH jAtaH|

paNDA

oDishA-taH lokasabhA sadasyaH bIjU janatA-dala netA ch beyajaMta jaya paMDA adya dalAt
tyAgapatraM pradattavAn| kendrapADA janapadAt sAMsadena etena svIya-tyAgapatraM dala-pramukhAya-
oDishA mukhyamantriNe ch navIna-paTanAyakAya samarpitam‌| tena proktaM yat‌ tyAga-patra-nirNayaH tena
duHkhena kRtaH| etasya nirNayasya sUchnA asau shIghrameva lokasabhAdhyakSAya pradAsyati|

uttarakoriyA-amerikA

amerikIya rASTrapatiH DAnalDa-drampaH pratipAditavAn yat uttara-koriyA netrA kima-
joMga-una varyeNa samaM prastAvitopaveshanam‌ anuSThAtuM amerikIya-dalamekaM uttara-koriyAM
samprAptam asti| videsha-vibhAgena proktaM yat ubhayoH deshayoH shiSTamaNDala-starIya sambhASaNaM
sainya-rahita-kSetre pAnamunajoma-grAme bhavati|

mekunu jhanJjhAvataH

dakSiNa-omAna-yamane sokotrA dvIpe ch bhISaNa-mekunu jhanJjhAvAtena ekAdasha janAH
mRtAH, eteSu bhAratIyAH api samAviSTAH santi| omAnasya nAgarika-rakSA-sevayA chturNAM
janAnAM mRtyoH sUchnA pradattA| sokotrAyAH rAjyapAlena ramajI maharosa varyeNa sUchitaM yat dvIpe
mRtakAnAM saMkhyA sapta-saMjAtA, aSTa bhAratIya-nAvikAH api viluptAH sUchyante|

pAkistAnaH

pAkistAnena AgAmi mAsasya panJchviMshe divase nirvAchnAt‌ prAk‌ pUrva-pradhAna-
nyAyAdhIshaH nAsirula-mulkaH antarima-pradhAnamantrI niyukto'sti| pratipakSa-netA khurshIda-shAhaH
adya islAmAbAde vArtAhara-sammelane idaM pratipAditavAn| ghoSaNAnantaraM abbAsI varyasya
pAkistAna-muslima-lIga-navAjdala-shAhasya pAkistAna-pIpalsa pArTI dalayormadhye saMjAta-

saMgharSo'pi samApto jAtaH|

bhArata-bAMglAdeshaH

bAMglAdeshasya sI.esa.AI.Ara. iti rASTriya-sAmudrika saMsthAnasya shiSTamaNDalastarIya-
dalaM adya prAtau govA-nagare samprAptam‌| sammelanamidaM vaijnJAnika-audyogika-anusandhAna
samityAH atha ch bAMglAdesha-samityAH madhye sahayogaM vivardhituM sanJjAyate| bAMglAdesha-pakSataH
ashoka-kumAra bisvAsaH vArtAharaiH sUchitavAn| bisvAsaH dhanyavAdaH jnJApitaH yat bhAratasya
sahayogaM vinA bAMglAdeshaH vikAsakartuM na shakyate|
