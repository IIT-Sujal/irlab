jammU AkramaNam‌

jammU-kashmIre jammU ityasya suMjavAnakSetrasya sainyasthAtre
vihitAtaGkavAdyAkramaNe sainyabalAdhikAridvayaM panJchtvaGgatam| yadyapi nAgarikaikaM
sametya chtvAro vraNitAH sUchyante| rAjyasyArakSi-mahAnirIkSakeNa esa DI siMhena
saMsUchitaM yat ghaTanAsthalaM surakSAkarmibhiH svAdhInaM kRtamasti| ghaTaneyaM prAtaH
chturvAdine vihitA| surakSAkAraNAni manasi nidhAya ghaTanAsthalaM nikaSA vidyAlayAH
pihitAH santi| kendrIya-gRhamantriNA rAjanAthasihena janAH samAshvAsitAH yat
sainyabalAni tatra svIyamuttaradAyitvaM mahannibhAlayanti|

vittamantriNA arUNajeTalinA agrimavittavarSe vittIyasthitiH sudRDhA bhaviSyati
