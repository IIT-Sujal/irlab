vastusevAkarapariSadaH - upaveshanam

vittamantriNaH aruNa-jeTalinaH AdhyakSe vastusevAkara-pariSadaH upaveshanam‌ adya
guvAhATI-nagare samAyojyate| nUtanakara-vyavasthAyAH pravartanasya prathame trayamAsike prabhAva-
viSaye asmin‌ upaveshane samIkiSyate| asamasya vitamantrI hemanta-vishva-sharmA avravIt‌ yat‌
upaveshanamidaM mahatvapUrNamasti yato hi upaveshane'smin‌ nUtanakarapraNAlyAH prarvatanasya
mAsatrayAnantara asyAH pragateH samIkSA vidhAsyate|

meghAlayochchnyAyAlayaH

meghAlayojchnyAyAlayena rAjye saMsadIya-sachivAnAM niyukti-samabaddhaH
panJchAdhikadvisahasratamavarSIyaH adhiniyamaH naristIkRtaH| saptadasha-sachivAH padatyAgArthaM

uchchnyAyAlaya-vIvIpIeTI

AgAmi-vidhAnasabhA-nirvAchneSu vIvIpIedI-yantra-sauvidhyAnvita-vaidhudANavika-
matadAna-yantrANAM viSaye matadAtRNAM madhye jAgarukatA-prasAraNArthaM vihita-kAyachiraNa-viSaye
shapathapatraM prastotuM gujarAtochchnyAyAlayena mukhyanivachinAdhikArI nirdiSTaH| viSaye'smin
prastutAyAM janahitayAchikAyAM vAdashravaNaM vidadhatA nyAyAlayena etadartha nirdiSTam|

prashAsanam‌ - plANDuH

vipaNau samuchita-mUlyeSu palANDUnAm‌ upalabdhatAM sunishchetuM mUlya-sthirIkaraNa-
koSaprabandha-samityA shAsakIyAbhikaraNaiH palANDUnAm‌ AyAtaM kartuM nirNItamasti| gatadine
navadillyAM upabhoktA-viSaya-vibhAgIyena sachivena ekasmin‌ upaveshane nirNayo'yaM vihitaH|

rASTrapatiH-madhyapradeshayAtrA

rASTrapatiH rAmanAtha-kovindaH dvidivasIya-madhyapradeshayAtrA prasaMge adya bhopAlaM
samprApsyati| asmadIyo vArtAharaH sUchyati yat rASTrapati-padabhAra-grahaNAnantaraM shrI
kovindasya iyaM prathamA madhyapradesha-yAtrA vartate| rASTrapatiH adya bhopAle samAyojyamAne
kAryakramadvaye upasthAsyati| lAla-pareDa-grAUNDa-sthale samAyojyamAne kabIra-prakaTotsava-
kAryakrame rASTrapatiH bhAgaM grahiSyati|
avasere'smin panJchdasha-shatAbdasya rahasyavAdi-kavi-viSayakasya rAjyaprashAsanasya
saMskRtivibhAga dvArA prakAshitasya pustakasya vimochnaM rASTrapatinA vidhAsyate| asau bhopAle
vIrAMgaNAyAH jhalakarI-bAI-ityasyAH pratimAyAH anAvaraNamapi vidhAsyati|

rijarvabaiMkam‌-variSThanAgarikAH

trisahasra-prakaraNeSu anvIkSaNaM vidhIyate| gatadine ekayA AdhikArika vijnJpyA nigaditaM yat‌
anena abhirakaraNena eteSAM prakaraNAnAM shaMsayAtmakaH pakSaH api anvIkSiSyate|
