pravartananidishAlayaH kArtiH

eyarasela-maiksis prakaraNe pravartana-nideshAlayena adya kAMgresa-netuH
pI..chidambaramasya tanayasya kArtichidambaramasya chennaI atha ch dillIsthe parisareSu
sahasAnvIkSaNAni vihitAni| kArti-chidambaramasya vAkkIlenoktaM yat‌
pravartananideshAlayena anveSaNAvadhau prAtrANi naiva adhikRtAni|

karnATake basa durghaTanA

karnATakarAjye hasanakSetre rAjya/rivahananigamasya basayAnadurghaTanAyAm‌ aSToM
janAH mRtAH, dasha janAshch vraNitAH| yAne chtvAriMshat yAtriNaH AruDhAH Asan|
rAjyasya parivahanamantrI ech.ema. revannA mRtakAnAM parijanebhyaH trilakSarupyakANAm
anugraharAshiH udghoSayat|

mahArASTre kArayAnadurghaTanA
mahArASTrasya sAMgalI janapade adya prAtaH kArayAnadurghaTanAyAM paMchmalla-
krIDakAn sametya SaD janAH mRtAH| sapta janAshch vraNitAH sUchyante|
