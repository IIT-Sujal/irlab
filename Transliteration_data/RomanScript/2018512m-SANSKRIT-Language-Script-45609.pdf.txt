pradhAnamantrI nepAlayAtrA

pradhAnamantrI shrInarendramodI, nepAla-pradhAnamantrI khaDga-prasAda-olI-sharmA ch
gatadine kAThamANDU-nagare pratinidhi-maNDalastarIyaM mantraNopaveshanam anuSThitavantau|
upaveshane'smin dvipAkSika-sambandhAnAM sarveSu AyAmeSu vyApikA mantraNA sanJjAtA|
sahaiva, dvayoH deshayoH sambandhAnAM sudRDhIkaraNAya ubhAbhyAM netRbhyAM pratibaddhatApi
punarvyaktIkRtA| mantraNAnantaraM shrImodi-olibhyAM arUNa-tri ityAkhyAyAH
jalavidyut‌-pariyojanAyAH shilAnyAso'pi vihitaH| svIye preSa-vaktavye modinA
proktaM yat bhArata-nepAtrayoH maitrIsambandhAH prAshAsanika-sambandhAn‌ utkramya sudUra
vistRtAH vidyante| anena proditaM yad ime sambandhAH desha-dvayasya janAnAM madhye
sauhArdamayAH gahanatamAshch vartante| pradhAnamantriNA bhaNitaM yad bhArataM nepAlasya
Rddhaye siddhaye ch sarvadA sahayogaparaM vartate| shrImodI nyagAdIt yat saH atIva
vishiSTe samaye nepAlaM paryaTati, yadA nepAle-saMghIya gaNatantrasya sthAnIya-nikAyAnAM
ch nirvAchnAni sAphalyena samAyojitAni| anenoktaM yad bhArataM nepAlasya
abhyunnataye sarvadA skandhena skandhaM saMyojya kAryamAchriSyati|

eshiyA-janasanJchAra-sammelanam

pradhAnamantriNA shrInarendramodinA bhaNitaM yat eshiyA-mahAdvIpaM janasanJchAra-
vyApArasya navoditaM bhaviSNu-kSetraM vidyate, api ch, kSetramidam antArASTriya-
sahayogasya avasarAn prastauti| gatarAtrau navadillyAM sampanne eshiyA-janasanJchAra-
sammelane preSite svIya-sandeshe shrImodI nyagAdIt‌ yat‌ bhAratena prastUyamAne
antArASTriya-sahayoge prAvidhika-sahabhAgitA, sarvasamAveshi-janasanJchArAya saphala-
kAryanItishch samAdiSTe staH| anena mAdhyamena vayaM samagrakSetre sanJchAra-mAdhyamAnAM
lokatantrasya ch suhRDhaThIkaraNa samIhAmahe| samprati pradhAnamantrI nepAlayAtrA-rato vartate|
sanJchAra-sammelane tasya sandeshaH ch eshiyA-prashAnta-mahAsAgarIya-prasAraNa-vikAsa-
saMsthAnasya e.AI.bI.DItyasya nideshakena “chAMga jiga” ityamunA paThitvA sashrAvitaH|
