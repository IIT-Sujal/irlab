pradhAnamaMtrI

pradhAnamaMtrI narendramodI vishvasiti yat bhArataM vANijya saralI kartRNAM
vaishvikasUchyAM Agami varSe vastusevAkarasya Agamanena itopi upari -
sthAnam avApsyati| asaugatadine navadillyAm vANijya -bhArata-

-kAryakramaM sambodhayati sma| shrI modI avocht yat vANijya -saMshodhana
saralIkaraNaH vishvasUchyAM bhAratena dvAchtvAriMshat‌ sthAnAni atikramya -
shatasthAneSu sthAnaM prAptamasti| eSA sUchI maI mAsasya anusAraM vartate|

rASTrapatiH chattIsagaDha

rASTrapatiH rAmanAtha kovindaH adya Arabhya dvidivasI
chattIsagaDhayAtrAM kariSyati| asau tatra rAyapurasthe shahIdI-smArakasthale
shraddhAMjaliH samarpayiSyati| tataH paraM rASTrapatiH nayArAyapuranagare chattIsagaDha़-
rAjyotsavakAryakramaM saMbodhayiSyati, rASTrapateH padagrahaNAnantaraM kasyApi
rAjyasya prathameyaM yAtrA vartate|
paMjAba abhiyAna
