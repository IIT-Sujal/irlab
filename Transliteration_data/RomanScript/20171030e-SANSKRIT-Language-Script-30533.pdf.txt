uparASTrapatiH

uparASTrapatinA ema.veMkaiyAnAyaDunA proktaM yat‌ sashaktaH samAjaH
saMchAramAdhyamAni ch bhraSTAchAraM virudhya pravartite yuddhe mahattvapUrNa-bhUmikAM
nirvoDhuM shaknuvanti| adya navadillyAM satarkatA-jAgaruka-saptAhasya
udghATanakrame shrInAyaDunA bhaNitaM yat satyatayA-niSThayA ch sAmAjika-
  sudRDhIbhavanti|

bhArata-saMyukta arA! amIrAta sahabhAgitA

bhArata-saMyukta-araba-amIrAta sahabhAgitA-shikharopaSaveshanam adya dubaI
nagare samArabdham| asyoddeshyaM deshayormadhye vANijya-niveshavardhanaM vartate|
samupaveshanasya viSayaH “niveshaH kriyAnvanam' cheti vartate| asminnupaveshane
vichArayiSyate yat kathaMkAraM bhAratasya mukhya pariyojanAsu saMyukta-araba-
amIrAtasya vANijyikAH niveshakAshch sahAyyamupapAdayitumarhiSyanti|
sammelane svAsthya-nibhAlanam‌, paryaTanam‌, shikSA, AdhArikasauvidhyam‌
chetyAdi viSayeSu savisheSaM mantraNA bhaviSyati|
