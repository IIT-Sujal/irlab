mukhyavArtAH

 uchchtamanyAyAlayena kendrIya-sashastra-balAnAM chturNA samUhAnAM dArjiliMga-kSetrAt
pratyArtanaM svIkRtam|

pAkistAnasya vidhi mantriNA svapadAt tyAgapatraM pradattam|

bhAjapA-kAMgresa-sUchI

bhAjapA dalena gujarAta-vidhAnasabhA-nirvAchnAnAM kRte pratyAshinAM SaSTha-sUchI
adya prakAshitA| atrAntare kAMgresa dalenApi tRtIyA antimA ch pratyAshinAM sUchI
prasAritA asti| uttarIya-madhya-gujarAte dvitIya-chraNAntargataM tri-navatiH AsanAnAM
kRte nAmAkana-patra-pajIkaraNasyAdaya antimaH divasaH AsIt| prathama chraNAya
AgAmimAsasya navadinAke, chturdashe ch dvitIya chraNAya matadAnaM bhavitA| matagaNanA
himAchlapradeshena sahaiva aSTAdashe dinAMke bhavitA|

rAjye prachArAbhiyAnaM sajavaM prachlati| prathama chraNe saurASTra-kachcha-dakSiNa-
gujarAtasya navadasha-janapadeSu matadAnaM bhaviSyati| pradhAnamantriNA narendramodinA adya
bhuja-kSetre prachArAbhiyAnaM prArabdham| tena bhaNitaM yat gujarAte bhAjapAdalasya
vikAsavAdasya kAMgresa dalasya vaMshavAdasya ch madhye svardhA vartate|

uchchtama nyAyAlayaH

uchchtamanyAyAlayena pashchima baMgAlasya ashAMta dArjiliMga-kalimapoMga-
janapadAbhyAM kendrIya-sashastra-balAnAM pratyAvartana svIkRtam‌| samprati aSTasu chtvAraH
samUhAH nirvAchnAnAM kRte gujarAte niyokSyante|

rASTrapati

rASTrapatinA rAmanAtha-kovindena proktaM yat saMvidhAnasya saphalatA na kevalaM
prAvadhAneSu apitu janeSu rAjanItika-daleSu ch nirbharA vartate| adya navadillyAm
antArASTriya-ambeDakara-samAgame tena bhaNitaM yat prashAsanena saMvidhAne nihitAnAM
rAjyasya nIti-nirdeshaka-tatvAnAm‌ anurUpam‌ anusUchita-jAti-janajAtIya-samUdAyAnAM
mahilAnAM kRSakANAM ch sAmAjika-shaikSika-utthAnAya kAryaM karaNIyam|

kendram‌

kendraM pachAyatAnAM dvi-lakSa-mahilA-pratinidhInAM kSamatAM kaushalaM ch unnetuM
prashikSaNaM pradAsyati| kendrIya mahilA-bAla-vikAsa-mantrI menakAgAndhI adya
navadillyAM paMchAyatI-rAja-saMsthAnAM mahilA-pratinidhInAM kSamatA-vikAsa kAryakramam‌
udghATayan etaduktavatI|

pAkistAnaH

pAkistAne vidhimantriNA jAhida-hAmidena IshanindA-vidhau tathoktaM shaithilyaM
virudhya vyApaka-pradarshanAnantaraM svapadaM parityaktam| pradarshanakAriNAM mantriNaH
tyAgapatrameva pramukhA samIhA avartata| etasmAdeva kAraNAt islAmAbAda-rAvalapiMDI-
rAjamArgo'pi pihitaH AsIt‌|

jammU-kashmIraH

karoti|
