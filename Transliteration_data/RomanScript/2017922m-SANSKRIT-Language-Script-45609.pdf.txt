vidhAsyati| dvidivasIya-yAtrAvadhau asau naikAH pariyojanAH

sthale dInadayAla-hastakalA-saMkula iti vyApAra-sauvidhyakendraM rASTrAya
samarpayiSyati| vArANasI-sUrata-vaDodarA-nagareSu madhye mahamanA-drutagAmi-

pradhAnamantrI svachchatA-kAryakrameSu sahabhAgitvam AvakSyati| asau pashudhana-
