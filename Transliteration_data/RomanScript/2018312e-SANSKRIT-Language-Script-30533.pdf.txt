pradhAnamantrI phrAMsa rASTrapati

pradhAnamantrI narendramodI phrAMsa rASTrapatiH imainuela maikroM ch adya uttara pradeshe
mirjApurajanapadasya dAdarakalA-grAme ekottarashata-megAvAiTa-saurorjA-saMyatra
samudghATitavantau|| etat‌ deshasya vRhattamaM saurorjA-saMyaMtraM phrAMsadeshIya-samavAyena
IenajIAII ityanena saMsthApitamasti| deshayormadhye'nubandhAnusAraM bhAratIya-saurorjA-
nigamaH phrAMsa-samavAyAt‌ panJchviMshati-varSANi yAvad vidyut krayaNaM kariSyati|
avasare'smin mukhyamantrI yogI AdityanAthaH, aneke mahAjanAshch upasthitA
Asan| shrImodI shrI mekroM ch vArANasIM pratyAgatya tatra dInadayAla hastakalA saMkulam
apashyatAm‌|

mahAjana-varyayA sadanaM shvaH yAvat‌ sthagitam‌| rAjya sabhAyAmapi eAIeDIemake,
