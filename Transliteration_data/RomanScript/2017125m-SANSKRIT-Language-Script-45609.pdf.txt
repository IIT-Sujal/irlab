pradhAnamantrI-gujarAtaprachArAbhiyAnam

vibhAjanAropaiH sabhAkSiStam| gatadine gujarAsya bhAvanagare tenoktaM yat kAMgresadalena
AglAnAM vibhajya shAsanaM kuru nItiH svIkRtA, paraM gujarAtasya vikAsaH shAntyA
aikyena sadabhAvanasya ch abhavat| gujarAtasya matadAtAraH nairvAMchnika-gulikayA
prabhAvitAH naiva bhaviSyanti| aparatra kAgresopAdhyakSaH rAhula-gAMdhI avocht yat‌
