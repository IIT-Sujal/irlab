pradhAnantrI TvITa

 

pradhAnamantrI narendra modI adya navadillyAM kanADAyAH pradhAnamantriNA
jasTina-TraDo vayaiNa saha dvapAkSikaM sambhASaNaM kariSyati| ubhayormithaH

iti sambhAvyate| pradhAnamantriNA narendramodinA kanADAdeshasya pradhAnamantriNA
jasTin-TraDo-varyeNa saha sambhASaNAya autsukyaM prakaTitam| ekasmin TvITa-
saMdeshe pradhAnamantriNA shrI modinA AshA prakaTitA yat TraDo varyasya parivAreNa saha
tadIyA bhAratayAtrA AnandapradA abhAviSyat| tena TraDo varyasya parivAreNa saha ekaM
purAtanaM chitramapi prakAshitam|
