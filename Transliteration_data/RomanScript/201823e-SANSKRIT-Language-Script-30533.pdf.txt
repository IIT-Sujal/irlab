pradhAnamantrI

pradhAnamantrI shrInarendramodI adya guvAhATyAm asamAllAbhaH (eDavAnTeja asama) iti shIrSakaM vaishvika-niveshaka-
sammelanaM samuddhATitavAn‌| atrAvasare asau proktavAn yat prashAsanasya pUrvIya-gatividhInAM nItayaH (ekTa IsTa
nIti”) pUrvottararAjyAni manasi nidhAya nirmitAssanti| nItireSA bhAratasya pUrvottarakSetrANAM AsiyAnadeshAn
sametya anyadeshIyajanaiH sArdhaM pArasparika-samparka-vyAvasAyika-sambandha-saMvardhanasya mAdhyamarupatvena vidyate| amunA
etadapi vijnJApitaM yat asama-pradesho'yaM AsiyAnadeshAnAM kRte bhAratasya pramukhadvAro vidyate iti nAsti kevalaM
kathanameva apitu vaktavye'smin vyApakadRSTiH vartate| pUrvottararAjyAnAM vikAsena samagradeshasya vikAso bhaviSyati|
atrAntare pradhAnamantriNA etadapi vijnJApitaM yat kendraprashAsanaM samAnyajanAnAM jIvanastaronnayanadishi kRtasaMkalpaM
vidyate| Ayavyayapatre AyuSmAna-bhArata iti yojanAyAH samuddhoSeNa nirdhanajanAH susvAsthya-sevAbhiH lAbhAnvitAH
bhaviSyanti ityapi pradhAnamantriNA pratipAditam‌| divasaddvayAtmakakAryakramasyodeshyam‌ asamam‌ AsiyAnadeshebhyaH
bhAratasya pramukhamArgatvena pratisthApanaM vidyate|
