pradhAna-mantriNA narendra-modinA landana-nagare rASTra-maNDala-
shAsanAdhyakSANAm‌ upaveshane laghUnAM, dvIpIya-deshAnAM ch chintAsu avadhAnasya
AvashyakatA savisheSaM pratipAditA| bhArataM sammelanasya prathame divase etadarthaM
katichidupAyAn udghoSayat| upAyeSu sthAyI-vikAsa-koSe bhAratasya
yogadAnasya dviguNIkaraNam, taddesheSu laghu-pariyojanAnAM kAryAnvayanaM,

mukhyanyAyAdhIsha-mahAbhiyogaH

kAgresadalaM sametya katichid‌ dalanetRbhiH adya uparASTrapati
rAjyasabhAyAshch sabhApati veMkaiyA-nAyaDu sammelya pradhAnanyAyAdhIshasya
virodhe mahAbhiyoga-prastAvasya sUchnA vijnJApitA| navadillyAM kAMgresadalasya
variSTho netA gulAma-nabI-AjAdaH nyagadat‌ yat rAjyasabhAyAM sapta-
rAjanItika-dalAnAm‌ eka-saptati-sAMsadaiH prastAve hastAkSaritamasti| eteSu
sapta-pUrva-sAsadAH api santi| amunA bhaNitaM yat‌ rAjyasabhAyAM
mahAbhiyoga-prastAvamAnetuM nyUnAnnyUnaM paMchAshat sAMsadAnAM sAhamatyasya
AvashyakatA bhavati| atrAntare sarvochch-nyAyAlayenAdya etadviSaye
sAMsadAnAM sArvajanikaM vaktavyaM durbhAgyapUrNamiti pratipAditam|

atrAntare vittamantrI arUNajeTalI nyagadat yat kAMgresAdi-vipakSi-
dalAni pradhAnanyAyAdhIshaM virUdhya mahAbhiyogaM rAjanItikopakaraNatvena
upayojayanti| mahAbhiyoga-prastAvaM sandRbhya pratikriyAM kurvan shrIjeTalI
nyagadat‌ yadetat‌ pratishodhAchraNimava vartate|
