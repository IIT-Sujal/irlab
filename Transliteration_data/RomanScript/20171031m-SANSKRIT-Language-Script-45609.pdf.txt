sarvochchnyAyAlayaH

yAchikA-viSaye vAdashravaNaM mAsatrayAya sthagitam| anuchchede'smin jammU-kashmIrAya visheSa-
prAvadhAnaM vihitamasti|

rASTriyaikatA divasaH

rASTramadya saradAra-vallabha-bhAI-paTelasya jayantIM rASTriyaikatA-divasatvena paripAlayati|
tasya dvAchtvAriMshadadhikaikashataM jayantyavasare naike kAryakramAH samAyojyante| pradhAnamantrI narendra-
modI navadillyAM mejara-dhyAnachnda-krIDAMgaNe samAyojyamANe mukhyakAryakrame samupasthAsyati|
asau ‘RUN FOR UNITY’ kAryakramasya shubhArambhaM vidhAsyati|

pUrvakArmikAn chAtrAn krIDakAn kendrIyArdhasainya-balAni ch sametya prAyaH panJchdashasahasra-
janAH dhAvana-kAryakrame'smin sahabhAgitvam‌ AvakSyanti| videsheSu api kAryakramo'yaM
samAyojyate|

prashAsanaM-vaiGakAni

nirvAchnAyogaH

nirvAchnAyogena AgAmi-gujarAta-vidhAnasabhA nirvAchnArthaM rASTriya-dalAnAM kRte
prasAraNasya avadhiH nirdhArito'sti| svIya-Adeshe nirvAchnAyogena nigaditaM yat pratyekamapi
rASTriya-dalAya panJchchtvAriMshat nimeSAtmakaH avadhiH pradatto'sti| nirvAchAyogena saha parAmarshaM
vidhAya prasArabhAratI-nigamena etarthaM prasAraNa-tithiH nirdhArayiSyate|

bhUTAna-nareshaH

bhUTAna nareshaH jigme-khesara-nAmagyAla-vAMgachukaH chturdavasIya-bhArata yAtrA-prasaGge adya
navadillyAM samprApnoti| svIya-yAtrAvadhau asau rASTrapatiM rAmanAtha-kovindaM pradhAnamantriNaM
narendramodinaM ch meliSyati| nRpasya sammAne rAtribhojanaM samAyojayiSyate| anayA yAtrayA
deshadvaya-madhye dvipAkSika-sahayogaH bhUyo'pi sudRDhaH bhaviSyati|

prashAsanam‌-vastusevAkaraH

vastusevAkara-niyamasya antargatam‌ AyakaravivaraNaM prastotuM antamo'dhiH navambara
mAsIyaH triMshavidahivaso vihito'sti| itaH prAk‌ etadartham‌ akTUbara mAsasya ekatrishat‌-
divasaH antima divasatvena avartat|

rASTradhya prAktan-pradhAnamantriNaH indirAgAndhinaH trayastriMshattamAM puNyatithim upalakSya
tasyai shraddhAMjaliM samarpayati| navadillyAM tasyAH samAdhisthale shaktisthale netRbhiH gaNyamAnya-
janaishch shraddhAMjaliH samarpayiSyate| avasare'smin‌ naike kAryakramAH samAyojyante|
