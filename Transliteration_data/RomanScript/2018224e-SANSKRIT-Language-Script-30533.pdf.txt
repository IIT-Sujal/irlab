pradhAnamantrI-pariyojanA

pradhAna-mantriNA narendra-modinA adya kendrashAsite pradeshe damana-dIve eka-sahasra-
koTi-rupyakANAM vikAsa-pariyojanAH samudghATitAH| anena tatra udagra-vimAna-sevApi
samudghATitA| seveya damana-dIvayormadhye prachliSyati| shrImodinA uDa़Ana
yojanAntargatam eyara oDishA vimAna sevApi samArabdhA| sevayAnayA
ahamadAbAdanagarataH damana dIvaM samparkayiSyati| janasabhAM sambodhayatA shrImodinA
proktaM yat udagravimAna sevAmAdhyamena vimAnasevA samparkeNa ch etat kendrashAsita-
kSetraM vikAsasya mukhyadhArAyAM samAviSTaM bhaviSyati| anenoktaM yat samparkasamedhanena
paryaTanamapi vardhiSyati|

vittamaMtrI-pI.ena.bI.

vittamantriNA aruNajeTalinA panJjAba-neshanala-baike prachlantInAM pravanJchnAnAm
abhijnJAna-vaiphalyadRshA baiMka-niyAmakAH kaTvAlochitAH| vaishvika vANijya sammelane'
adya asau nyagadat‌ yat‌ pravanJchkaiH saha baika karmakarANAM saMliptiH chintAkarI vartate|
shrI jeTalinoktaM yat baiMka etAishAnAM luNThanakarmaNAm avarodhanAya
jAgarukaiH bhAvyam AsIt| anena saMketitaM yat AvashyakatAyAM satyAM pravanJchkAnAM
daNDArthaM vidhiniyamAH iDhIkariSyante| jeTalinA udyogajagataH AhavAnaM kRtaM yad tad
vANijya-vRttauM anaitikAchraNaM parityajet|

pravartana nideshAlayaH nIrava modI

pravartana-nideshAlayenAdya nIrava-modi-samUhasya trayovishatyuttara-panJchshata-koTi
rUpyakato5pyadhikA achlasampattayaH svAyattIkRtA| nideshAlrayena avaidha-dhanAharaNa-rodhi-
vidherantargataM tasya bhUri aTTAlikAH kRSyAvAsabhUmishch svAyatIkRtAH santi|

sushIlamodI-vastusevAkara vidheyakam

rAjya vittamantriNAM samUhaH bihAra-upamukhya-mantriNaH sushIlamodinaH netRtve
prastAvaM prAstauta yat apraila pratipadAtaH vastUnAM antArAjyIyaM nayanAnayanaM
ilekTronika mArga-patraka-mAdhyamena eva bhavet| anena vastUnAM nayanAnayanaM druta
bhaviSyati|

videshasachivaH - peDchiMga

videsha-sachivena vijaya-gokhale-varyeNa hayaHH peDchiga-nagare chIna-videshamantriNA
vAMga yI ityamunA saha bhUri prakaraNeSu sambhASaNaM kRtam| peichiMgasthena bhAratIya-
dUtAvAsena TvITa sandeshe adya etadviSaye saMsUchitam‌| dUtAvAsena nyagAdi yada
videsha-sachivasya chIna yAtreyaM deshayoH madhye hitakara-prakaraNAnAM kAryasUchIviSaye
parichrchAyai yojanAnAM vinimayArthaM aiSamo pradhAnamantriNaH chInayAtrAM ch saMdRbhya
sanJjAtA|

meghAlayaH AtaMkavAdI

surakSAbhaTaiH adya meghAlayasya sudikgre grAme sanJjAte saMgharSe gAro neshanala
libareshana ArmI ityasya svayambhU-samAdeST-pramukhaH sohana DI. shirA vyApAditaH|
asau gAro hilsa kSetre sakriyaH AsIt| rAjye parashvaH vidhAnasabhA-nirvAchnamAlakSya
gAro hilsa kSetre surakSAbhaTaiH AtaMki kriyAkalApAn virudhya abhiyAnaM tIvrIkRtamasti|

shrIlaMkA-dhIvarAH
shrIlaMkA-prashAsanena nigaDitAnAM viMshatiH bhAratIya-dhIvarANAM vimochnasya nirNayaH
vihitaH| matsya vibhAgasya pravaktA avochta yat vimochnasyAdeshaH prasArito'sti|
