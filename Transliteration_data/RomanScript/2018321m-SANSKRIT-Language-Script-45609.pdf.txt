pradhAnamatrI-vishvavyApArasaghaTanam‌

pradhAnamantrI narendramodI samAveshi-sidadhAntAdhAritAM sarvasammatAM ch bahupakSIyAM
vyApAra-vyavasthAM pravartituM svIya-pratibad‌dhatAM punaH udIritavAn‌| shrImodI gatadine
navadillyAM vishvavyApArasaMghaTanasya mantristarIyam‌ anaupachArika sammelanaM
sambodhayati sma| pradhAnamantriNA proktaM yat‌ bahupakSIya-vyApAvyavasthAyAH samAhavAnasya sammukhIkaraNaM mahatvapUrNamasti|

vishvavyApArasaMghaTanam‌-prabhuH

vANijyodyogamantrI suresha-prabhuH nyagadat yat‌ pravartamAna-vyApAra-sandarbhe
vishva-vyApAra-saMghaTanaM bhUyo'pi mahatvapUrNa sanJjAtamasti yatra hi kaishchit-deshaiH
ekapakSIya-kAryAchraNArthaM prayAso vidhIyate atha ch idaM pratyekamapi deshAya hAnikaraM
setsyati| gatadine navadillyAM sanJchAra-mAdhyamAni sambodhayan shrI prabhuH vyAharat
yat pratyekamapi deshe svIya-vichArAn prastotuM svataMtra-niSpakSa-rItyA ch parichrchA
jAyate|

kRSi-kaushalam‌

kRSimantrAlayena kaushalavikAsa-mantrAlayena ch kRSikSetre sahayogikSetre ch
vyApArakSamatAM saMvardhituM sahasamatipatrANi hastAkSaritAni| anena sva-vRttitA-kSetreSu
grAmINa-yUnAM mahilAnAM kushalajanAnAM kRte ch protsAhanam avApsyate| gatadine
kRSimantriNaH rAdhAmohana-siMhasya kaushalavikAsa-mantriNaH dhaMrmendra-pradhAnasya ch
upasthitau etAni sandhipatrANi hastAkSaritAni|

sarvochchnyAyAlayaH sUchnAdhikAraH

sUchnAyAH adhikArasya antargataM sUchnAm‌ adhigantuM uchchnyAyAlayaiH pravartitaM
shulkam‌ sarvochchnyAyAlayena pratyAvedanaM panJchAshat‌-rUpyakAtmikA uchchtamasImA
nirdhAritesti| sUchnAdhikAra-kAryakartRNAM kRte ayaM prasannatAyAH viSayausti|
nyAyamUrtinAm‌ e ke goyala-Ara epha narImana- yU yU lalita ityeteSAM khaNDapIThena
sUchnAyAHH kAraNaM prakaTIkartuM AvedakAn naiva vivashIkartuM uchchnyAyAlayAH nirdiSTAH|
