aSTottara-dvi sahasratamavarSe mumbayyAM durApannAtaMkyAkramaNasya navaH-varSapUrtau
tatra hutiGgatebhyaH rASTrasya vinamra-shraddhAnJjaliH|

pradhAnamantriNo rASTrasambodhanam

pradhAna mantriNA shrInarendramodinA AtaMkavAdaM nirmUlayituM sambhUya kAryAchraNam
Avashyakam‌ iti pratipAditama| anena proktaM yat‌ AtaMkavATaH sAmAjika-saMrachnAM
durbalIkurute, samAjaM vidhvaMsayituM ch prayatate| AkAshavANyAH mana kI bAta iti
kAryakrame shrImodinA bhaNitaM yat‌ AtaMkavAdamugravAdashch utkhAtAyituM na kevalaM bhAratenaiva
apitu sarvaishchApi mAnavatAvAdishaktibhiH saMdhaishch sAvadhAnaiH prayatnaparaishch bhAvyam‌|
pradhAnamantriNA AtaMkavAdaH samasta-vishvAya saMkaTakaraH pratipAditaH
mumbayyAtaMkavAdyAkramaNe mRtakAn‌ janAn‌ saMsmaratA anenoditaM yad rASTraM teSAM
balidAnaM na kadApi vismariSyati| adya saMvidhAnadivasAvasare shrImodI nyagadat‌ yat‌
samAnatA saMvedanashIlatA ch asmAkaM saMvidhAnasya sarvotkRSTa-pakSadvayaM vartate|
AgAmi-mAsasya chturthe divase bhAratIya nau sainya-divasAvasaramupalakSya modinA
sainyabhaTAH adhikAriNashch bhUri bhUri shlAghAvachobhiHH vardhApitAH|

rASTrapatiH saMvidhAnam


rASTrapatiH rAmanAkovindaH pratyapAdayat yat bhAratIyasaMvidhAnaM sthiraM naiva apitu
sagatikamasti| asau navadillyAM saMvidhAnadivasAvasare samAyojite kAryakrame
abhibhASate sma| shrIkovindaH nyagadat yat yathA saMvidhAnaM janAn sashaktIkurute tathaiva
janAshchApi saMvidhAnaM sashaktaM kurvanti| anenoktaM yat‌ savidhAnam‌ amUrtavichAro
nAsti| rASTrapatinokta yat‌ sarvochch-nyAyapAlikAyAH samakSaM bRhat‌
samAhavAnamiTamasti yat‌ kathaM vAdashravaNaprakriyA sajavaM sampadyeta iti|
asminnavasare pradhAna-nyAyAdhIshaH dIpakamishraH vidhimantrI ravishaMkaraprasAdashch
upasthitau AstAm‌|

gRhamantrI rUsa prasthAnam‌

gRhamantrI rAjanAthasiMhaH adya tridivasIya-rUsayAtrAyai samprasthitaH| yAtrAvasare
shrIsiMhaH rUsasya gRhamantriNaM blAdImIra koloktasephaM ch sametya anyaiH variSThanet-
bhissAkamapi vichAra-vimarshaM kariSyati| chrchAvasare dvaipAkSika-viSayaNAM sarveSvapi
vinduSu, antArASTriya-prakaraNeSu ch mantraNA kariSyate|

uttarapradesha- sthAnIyanikAyanirvAchnam


uttarapradeshe panJchvinshati-janapadeSu nAgarIya-sthAnIya-nikAyAnAM nirvAchnAnAM
dvitIya-chraNasya matadAnam adya Ayojitam| matadAnaM prAtaH saptavAdanAt sAyaM
panJchvAdanaparyanta prachlitamaH| gAjiyAbAda, alIgaDha़, mathurA, ilAhAbAda, lakhanaU,
vArANasI, chetyATIn‌ SaNNagara nigamAn‌, ekapanJchAshat‌ nagarapAlikA-pariSadaH,
dvAtrinshaduttara-ekashata-nagara panJchAyatAni ch sametya navAshItyadhika-ekashata-
sthAnIya-nikAyAnAH kRte adya matadAnaM sanJjAtam|

mumbayyAkramaNam‌

aSTottara-dvisahasratame varSe mumbayyAM durApanna-AtaMkyAkramaNe hutiGgatebhyaH
janebhyaH adya rASTreNa bhAvasaMvalitAH shraddhAnJjalayaH samarpitAH| asmAkaM vArtAharo
varNayati yad AkramaNasya navamyAM varSapUrtau rAjye naike kAryakramAH samAyojitAH|
AtaMkyAkramaNe'smin chtuSSaSTi-uttara-ekashataM janAH mRtyumupagatAH Asan|
trishatAdhikAshch vraNitAH Asan| asminnavasare mahArASTrasya rAjyapAlena sI.
vidyAsAgara rAo varyeNa, mukhyamantriNA devendra-phaNanavIshena, kendrIyamantriNA haMsarAja
ahIreNa, kirena ririjunA, ch hutiGgatebhyaH vIrebhyaH shraddhAnJjalayaH samarpitAH|

pAkistAna-pradarshanam|

pAkistAne islAmAbAde, rAvala-piNDI nagare ch vigataikapakSAd virodha-ratAn
chramotkarSa-haThonmAda-vAdino virudhya vihite ArakSikAryAnvayane sanJjAta-dusthitiM
niyantrayituM sainyabalamAkAritamasti| dhArmikonmAdi-saMghaTanAnAM virodhAchraNa-kAraNena
prativeshi-nagaradvaye janajIvanaM bhRshaM duSprabhAvitamasti|

 

amerIkA-pAkistAna-saIda


amerIkAdeshena pAkistAnAya proktaM yat tat mumbayyAtaMkyAkramaNasya mukhya-
SaDayantrakAriNaM, pratibandhitasya 'jamAta-uda-dAvA' iti AtaMki-gulma-pramukhaM hAphija
saIdaM satvarameva nigaiya tasmin prakaraNaM panJjIkuryAt| amerIkayA pAkistAnaM
saMchetitaM yat‌ yadi tadetat‌ na kurute chedubhayoH deshayoH sambandhAH duSprabhAvitAH
bhaviSyanti| dhyeyamasti yat hAphija saIde amerIkayA eka-koTi-DAlara mUlyasya
upAyanarAshiH api apyuddhoSitamasti|
