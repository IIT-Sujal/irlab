mukhya-vArtAH

0 kendrIya-maMtri-maNDalena dravyasya khaNDa- vANijye svataH shata-pratishataM niveshaH
abhisvIkRtaH |

0 pradhAnamatrI prakhyAtArthashAstribhiH vividhakSetrANAM visheSajnJaishch  vichAra-vimarsha
karoti|

0 uchchtama-nyAyAlayaH tri-sadasyIyaM visheSa-anvIkSaNaM dalaM nirmAsyati, yad
hi pUrva-pradhAnamatriNaH iMdirA-gAMdhinaH hatyAnantaram‌ upadrava-prakaraNAn
anvISayiSyati|
 

pradhAnamantrI

pradhAnamaMtrI narendra modI nItyAyoge pramukhArthashAstribhiH akhila deshasya
vibhinna-kSetrebhyaH visheSajnJaiH ch sAkaM vichAraM vinimayate| Arthika kSetrAt-kSetra
visheSajnJAshch nItyAyogena pradhAnamaMtriNA sAkaM sambhASaNArthaM samAhUtAH| asmadIya
vArtAhareNa vijnJApitaM yat visheSajnJAnAM vichArAH deshasya Arthika-nIteH sudRDhIkaraNe
mahatvapUrNAH bhaveyu| vichAra-vimarsho'yaM Arthika-saMtulanaM-kRSi-vikAsa-nagara-vikAsa-
saMsAdhana-samparka-svAsthya shikSAdi kSetrANi adhikRtya bhavati|

kendrIya mantrimaNDalam

kendrIya mantrimaNDalena pratyakSa-videshI-nivesha- nItyAM saMshodhanaM kRtamasti|
etena deshe pratyakSa-videshI-pUMjI-niveshaH sukaro bhaviSyati| samprati kasyApi
dravyasya khaNDa- vANijye nItyAM svataH shata-pratishata- videshI- nivesho bhavitum
arhiSyati| yo hi adyAvadhi navachtvAriSad‌ pratishatam‌ AsIt‌ tato'dhikashch
sarvakArasya anumatyA| nirmANa-vikAse'pi svataH shata-pratishata-niveshaH anumaMsyate|
vaidishika-vimAnana-samavAyaiH anumatipuraHsaraM bhAratasya eyara-DNDiyA samavAye
ekonapanJchAshat pratishataM niveshaH shakyate|

rASTrapatiH

rASTrapatinA rAmanAtha kovindena bhaNitaM yat bhAratIya-rela rASTra-vikAsAya
rASTriya sevA saMsthAnaM vidyate| adya rASTrapati bhavane vibhin‍na-relavibhAgIya-
sevAnAM parIvIkSAdhInAdhikarAriNaH saMbodhayan‌ asau proktavAn yat rela-vibhAgaH
varSadvaya-pUrvam aSTakharba yAtrIn avahat| shrI kovindaH uktavAn yat rela jAlaH
deshasya saMskRti-paramparayoH saMvardhane sahAyatAM karoti|

 

 

uchchtama nyAyAlayaH

uchchtama nyAyAlayena proktaM yat pUrva pradhAnamantrI iMdirA gAndhinaH
chturashItyuttara-navadasha tame varSe hatyAyAH samanantaraM sikkha virodhI upadravANAm
anvIkSaNAya tri-sadasyAtmakasya vishiSTAnvIkSaNa- dalasya nirmANaM bhaviSyati|
etadartha mukhya nyAyAdhIshasya dIpakamishrasya AdhyakSyutena pIThena kendraM dalasya
virachnAya kechna nAmAnAM pradAnArthaM parAmRSTam dalasya adhyakSaH uchchnyAyAlayasya
pUrva nyAyAdhIsho bhavitA| nyAyAlaya-niyukta-samityA etadudghATitam yat
ekachtvAriMSaduttara dvishata prakaraNebhyaH vinaivAnusandhAnaM SaDashItyuttaraikashataM
prakaraNAni samAptanyAsan‌|

 karagila- shaitayam‌

karagila kSetre atyadhika shaityaM vartate| tatra nyUnatama-tApamAnaH shUnyAt‌ SaDa-
dashamalava trayoviMshati-adhaH aMkitaH atha adhikatama tApamAnashch shUnyAt sArdha-
saptamitaM nIchaiH AsIt| tatratyAH surU nadyAH jalaM himavat bhUtvAnantaraM
svalpapravahaNAn viTyutApUrte'pi samasyA samAgatA'sti| sthAnIya-janAapi etena
kAraNena duSprabhAvitAH santi| aparatra jammU-kashmIre'pi atyadhika-shaityAt‌
nyUnatamatApamAnaH shUnyAt‌ tri dashamalava-SaDa़ DigrI- adhaH samaMkitaH|

 

 chIna-jaDgamadUrabhASaH

 vishvasya smArTa jaDgamadUrabhASa-vipaNyAM chInadeshaH mandAyate| chInadeshIya-
sUchnA samparka-pravidhi-akAdamI pratipAditavAn yat chInadeshena gatavarSe kevalAni
sahasrAdhika nUtana-jaDgamadUrabhASasaMskaraNAni avatAritAni|
