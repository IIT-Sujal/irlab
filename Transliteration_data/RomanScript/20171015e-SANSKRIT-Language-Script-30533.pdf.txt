nItyAyogaH vRddhiH

nItyAyogasya upAdhyakSaH rAjIvakumAraH vyAharat yat trayodashottara-dvisahasnatame varSe
Arabdham Arthika mAndyaM nyUnatamastare vartate atha ch aSTAdashAdhika-dvisahasatam varSaM
yAvat‌ sakalagRhayotpAdanaM pratishataM SaDdashamalava navamitato pratishataM saptamitaM
bhavitum‌ arhiSyanti| nItyAyogasya upAdhyakSeNa pratipAditaM yat‌ saptottara-
dvisahasratamavarSAt trayodashAdhika-dvisahasnatamavarSa yAvat‌ rASTrasya pragatiH bhadratarA
avartata paraMch tatpashchAt‌ mAndyam‌ sammukhIkRtam‌|

rASTrapatiH kalAmaH
 

rASTrapatiH rAmanAthakovindaH avocht yat rASTranirmANe pUrvarASTrapateH DA. e.pI.je.
abdulakalAmasya yogadAnaM rASTreNa kadApi naiva vismArayiSyate| navadillyAM
rASTrapatibhavane DA. kalAmasya SaDashItitame jayantI samArohe rASTrapatinA proktaM yat
DA. kalAmasya vichArAH deshasya bhAvi santatiM prerayiSyanti| shrI kovindena SoDaSa
rAjyeSu DA. kalAmasya vichArANAm prasAraNe saMlagnasaMdeshavAhinIti yAnasya svAgataM
kRtam| atrAvasare prAdhAnamantriNa narendramodinA TvIT saMdesha mAdhyamena DA. kalAmAya
shraddhAMjaliH samarpitaH|

kRSiHmahilA

kRSimantrI rAdhAmohanasiMhaH pratyapAdayat‌ yat mahilA-kRSakAH deshaM
