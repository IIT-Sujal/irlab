jammU-bIjevAIema

bhAjapAdalasya yuvA saMghaTanena jammUkashmIre AtaMkavAda pravartanArtham‌
pAkistAnaM bhRshaM vininditam‌| bhAratIyajanatAyuvAmochau dvArA bhaNitaM yat‌
pAkistAnena bhAratasya sahanashIlatAyAH parIkSA na karaNIyA| yuva-sammelanaM

uchchtamanyAyAlayaH

 

uchchtamanyAyAlayena gujarAta-prashAsanaM chtussaptAhAbhyantare bilakIsa-bAno-
sAmuhika-kukarma- prakaraNe kAryAchraNa viSaye vivaraNaM pradAtuM vinirdiSTam| pramukha
nyAyAdhIshasya dIpaka mishrasya-AdhyakSe nyAyAdhIshatrayasya khaNDapIThena asmin
prakaraNe sAhAyya-rAshi- saMvardhanArthaM pRthak yAchikAyAH paMjIkaraNasyAnumatiH pradattA|
ghaTanAyAmasyA tasyAH parivArasya sapta sadasyAH hatAH Asan‌|

himAchlapradeshaH

himAchlapradeshe vidhAnasabhA-nirvAchnAnAM kRte nAmAkanasya adya antimo
divaso AsIt‌| aSTa-SaSTi-sadasyAtmikAyAH vidhAnasabhAyAH nirvAchnam‌ AgAmi
mAsasya navame dinAMke bhavitA|

samAjavAdI pArTI

gujarAtavidhAnasabhA nirvAchneSu samAjavAdI-pArTItidataM kAMgresadalasya samarthanaM
vidhAsyati| dalasya rASTriya-adhyakSeNa akhilesha yAdavenAdya lakhanaUnagare
ghoSaNeyaM vihitA| tena proktaM yat tadIyaM dalaM paMchAsaneSu nirvAchnasamare
avatariSyati|

kAMgresa-bIjepI

kAMgresadalena AropitaM yat bhAjapAdalena pATIdAra-netRbhyaH utkoch-pradattamiti
kAMgresa pravaktrA manISa tivAriNA bhaNitaM yat nistejaH bhAjapAdalaM yenakenaprakAreNa
vidhAnasabhA-nirvAchneSu vijayam ichchati|

 jApAna-nirvAchnam

pradhAnamantriNA narendramodinA jApAnasya pradhAnamantrI shijo Abe saMsadIya-
nirvAchneSu sAphalyArtham abhinanditaH| gatadine anuSThite madhyAvadhi-nirvAchne
Abe varyasya dalena nimnasadane dUtRtIyAMshaM bahumatam adhigatam|

videshamantrI

videshamantrI suSamA svarAjaH bAMglAdesha yAtrAyAH antime divase'dya
DhAkAyAM bhAratIya-uchchAyogasya nUtana-parisarasya shubhArabhaH vihitaH| tayA DhAkAyAH
bAridhArA- kSetre vistRta-parisarasya udghATanaM sikthavartikAM prajjvAlya vihitam|
videshamantriNA bhAratasya-Arthika-sahAyatayA prachlitAH paMchdasha-pariyojanAH api
samudghATitAH|

rAjanAtha-sihaH
