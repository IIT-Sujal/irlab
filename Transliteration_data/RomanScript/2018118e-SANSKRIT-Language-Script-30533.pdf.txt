pradhAnamantrI

pradhAnamantrI narendramodI-isAila pradhAnamantrI beMjAmina netanyAhU ch
adya gujarAta-yAtrAyAM vartete| tatra ahamadAbAda ubhau netArau Ayojite mArga-
prayANe sammilitau| sAbaramati Ashrame netArau, mahAtmAgAndhinaM shradadhAjaliM
samarSitavantau| ahamadAbAde devadholerAyAM netRbhyAm udyamitA- pravidhi
antArASTriya-kendraM nirIkSitam| bhArata-isrAila-deshayoH pragADh-bandhutvasya
chihana rUpeNa ekaH stambho'pi samudghATitaH|

suSamA svarAja-rAyasInA

videshamantriNA suSamA svarAjenokta yat AtaMkavAdaH sarvavidha-
vidhvaMsAnAM mAtA asti| shrImatI svarAjaH adya nava dillyAM rAyasInA-
saMvAde avadat yat gata dashakeSu AtaMkavAdaM prati asmad-vichArAH vikasitAH
kintu asmin DijiTala-yuge'pi AtaMkavAdAt hAniH gambhIrAyate, yato hi
anena kaTTaravAdasya druta prasArasya sambhAvanA vartate|

amerikA-saMyuktarASTram‌
