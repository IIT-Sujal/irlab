mukhyavArtAH

 pradhAnamantriNA narendramodinA bhAratIya- vANijya-udyoga-maMDala parisaMghasya phikkI
ityasya vArSika-sAmAnya-sabhAM saMbodhayan uktavAn yat IdRshI vyavasthA nirmIyate
yA na kevalaM pAradarshinI apitu sarveSAm prayojanAnAM dhyAnamapi rakSiSyati|

 prashAsanena AdhAra-patrasya saMyojanArtha AgAmi mArchmAsasya ekatrishaH divasaH
antima tithiH nirdhAritA'sti|

 dillyAH ekena visheSa-nyAyAlayena jhArakhaNDasya pUrva-mukhyamantrI madhukoDA kendra-
prashAsanasya pUrva-kRSNAMgAra-sachivaH ech sI guptA ch kRSNAMgAra-bhaSTAchAra prakaraNe
doSI-siddhau|

pradhAnamantrI-phikkI

pradhAnamantriNA narendramodinA navadillyAM bhAratIya- vANijya-udyoga-maMDala
parisaMghasya phikkI ityasya vArSika-sAmAnya-sabhA saMbodhitA| shrImodinA proktaM yat
prashAsanaM nirdhana-janAnAM sarvAH AvashyakatAH prapUrayituM vyavasthA saMrachyati|
pradhAnamantrI uktavAn yat sA vyavasthA na kevalaM pAradarshinI apitu sarveSAm
prayojanAnAM dhyAnamapi rakSiSyati|

uchchtama-nyAyAlayaH -AdhAraH

prashAsanena AdhAra-patrasya saMyojanArthaM AgAmi mArchmAsasya ekatriMshaH divasaH
antima tithiH nirdhAritA'sti| atrAntare shIrSa-nyAyAlaye AdhArapatra-saMyojanasya
anivAryatAM viruddhaya sthaganaM prArthayatInAM yAchikAnAM shravaNaM paMch-nyAyAdhIshAnAM
saMvidhAna pITha shvaH vidhAsyati|

gujarAta-nirvAchnam‌

gujarAte shvaH antima-paryAya-vidhAnasabhA-nirvAchnamAlakSya sarvavidha-sajjAH
prapUritAH| tatra tri-navatiH AsanAnAM kRte prAtaH aSTavAdanAt sAyaM paMchvAdanaM yAvat
matadAnaM bhavitA|

jammU-kashmIra-abhiyAnam‌

jammU-kashmIre bANDIporA-kupavADA-janapadayoH niyanyatrarekhAyAH pArshve durgama-
parvatIya-kSetre hyaH viluptAnAM pach-sainikAnAM anveSaNaM nirantara prachlati| sthala-senA-
pravaktA AkAshavANI saMsUchitA yat kSetrasya durgamatA jalavAyushch anveSaNe bAdhakau
staH| tenoktaM yat uddhAra-dalAni api tatra niyojitAni santi|

kRSNAMgAra-pravaMchnAkANum‌

dillyAH ekena visheSa-nyAyAlayena jhArakhaNDasya pUrva-mukhyamantrI madhukoDA
kendra-prashAsanasya pUrva-kRSNAMgAra-sachivaH ech sI guptA ch kRSNAMgAra-bhaSTAchAra prakaraNe
doSI-siddhau| sIbIAI ityasya visheSa nyAyAdhIshena bharata-parAshareNa rAjyasya pUrva-
mukhya-sachivaH e ke basuH atha ch vini-lauhodyoga iti nijI-samavAyaH api doSI-
siddhau|

saMsada-AkramaNam‌

adya rASTraM SoDasha-varSa-pUrvaM saMjAte saMsadAkramaNe hutAtmanAm ArakSINAM balidAnaM
smarati| uparASTrapatinA ema vekeyA nAyaDunA, pradhAnamantriNA narendramodinA,
lokasabhAdhyakSayA sumitrAmahAjana varyayA, pUrvapradhAnamantriNA Daॉ manamohanasihena anyaiH
rAjanetRbhishch saMsada-bhavane hutAtma-vIrabhyaH shraddhA kusumAni samarpitAni|

sIbIAI

sIbIAI ityanena chattIsagaDhasya mantriNaH dvayoH mudhA-yauna-sIDI prakaraNayoH
anveSaNaM sambhAlitam| sIbIAI-dvArA rAjyasya loka-nirmANa-mantriNaH rAjesha-
munAtasya paridevanAdhAreNa patrakAraM vinoda-varmaNaM rAjya kAMgresAdhyakSaM bhUpesha bAghelaM ch
virUddhaya prakaraNaM paMjIkAritamasti|

leha-shrInagara-rAjamArgaH

leha-shrInagara-rASTriya-rAjamArgaH yAtAyAtAya disambaramAsasya dashama-divasAt
pihito'sti| upatyakAyAM himapAta-kAraNAt kharaDoMgalA-mArgaH api avaruddhaH| tatra
tApamAnaH parahyaH nishIthe shUnyAt ekadashamala-chturmitaH atha ch gata-rAtrau shUnyAt‌
sArdhaSaT mitaHH nIchaiH samaMkitaH|

amerikA-uttarakoriyA

amerikAdeshena prokta yat‌ asau uttara-koriyAdeshena sama paramANu-nirastrIkaraNa
viSaye pUrva-samayavinA sambhASayituM prastuto'sti| vAshiMgaTane ekasmin kAryakrame
amerikIya videshamantriNA reksa-Tilarasanena bhaNitaM yat sambhASaNasya lakSyaM uttara-
koriyAyAH pUrNa-rUpeNa paramANu nirastrIkaraNam vartate|

uchchtama-nyAyAlayaH -yUnITeka

uchchtama-~nyAyAlayena rASTiya-samavAya-vidhi-nyAyAdhikaraNasya saH AdeshaH
sthagitaH, yena kendra-prashAsanAya bhavana-nirmANa-samavAyasya yUnITeka-limiTeDa-ityasya
adhigrahaNasya anumatiH pradattA AsIt|
