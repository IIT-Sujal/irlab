bhAratena saMyukta-rASTra-surakSA-pariSad pAkistAne poSita-AtaGkavAdi-sthalAnAM
unmUlanAya avadhAnArtham adhyarthitam| atrAntare bhAratena pAkistAne AtaGkavAdinaH
sharaNapradAnasyApi Aropo vyavasthApitaH| saMyuktarASTrasya surakSApariSadi

akabarUddInaH proktavAn yat kevalaM aphagAnistAne shAntisamarthanaM nAsti paryAptam apitu
saMyuktarASTreNa sImApArataH AgatAnAM AtaGkavAdinAM viSaye'pi dhyAnaM pradAtavyamasti|

prashAsanam‌

videshamantrAlayena vijnJApitaM yat dAvosaprAnte pradhAnamantriNaH narendra modinaH vishva-
Arthika-manJchopaveshanAt pRthak pAkistAnIya-pradhAnamantriNA saha dvipakSIyopaveshanasya na
ko'pi kAryakramo vidyate| shrImodI manJchsyAsya pUrNasatraM sambodhayiSyati| videshamantrAlaye
Arthika-sambandha-sachivena vijaya-gokhalevaryeNa pratipAditaM yat upaveshane'smin desha-
vishvastare ch bhAratasyArthavyavasthAM adhikRtya pradhAnamantriNo vichAre dhyAnaM kendrIbhUto
bhaviSyati|

pradhAnamantrI dhanyavAdaH

pradhAnamaMtrI narendra-modI niryAta-niyantraNa-vyavasthA-samUhai bhAratasya praveshArthaM
asTreliyAdeshaM samUha-sadasyAn ch prati AbhAro vyAhRtavAn| svIya-TvIT-sandeshe
pradhAnamantriNA narendramodinA bhaNitaM yat vigatavarSadvayeSu bhArataM ema-pI sI Ara, vAsenAra
ityetAbhyAM sahaiva samprati asTrailiyA samUhe'pi pravesho labdhaH| etena etat tu sunishchitameva
yat‌ bhArataM paramANu-aprasAradishi dadapratijo vartate|
