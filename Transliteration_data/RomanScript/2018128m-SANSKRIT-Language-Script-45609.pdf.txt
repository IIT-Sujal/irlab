 mana kI bAta

pradhAnamantrI narendramodI adya prAtaH ekAdashavAdane AkAshavANyAH mana kI bAta ityisman‌
kAryakrame deshavAsinAM purato svavichArAn‌ prastoSyati| kAryakramasya aiSamaH prathamasaMskaraNaM
AkAshavANI dUradarshanayoH sarvAbhiH vAhinIbhiH prAsAriyaSyate| kSetrIya bhASASvApi kAryakramobhayaM
rAtrau aSTavAdanAt prasArayiSyate|

sarvadalIyopaveshanam‌

lokasabhAdhyakSayA sumitrA mahAjanavaryayA saMsadaH Ayavyayapatraka-satrAt pUrvam adya sAyaM
sarvadalIyopaveshanam‌  samAhUtamasti Ayavyayapatraka-satrasya suchArU sachAlanAya upaveshanesmin‌
chrchA bhaviSyati| rASTrapatiH rAmanAthakovindaH shvaH sadanadvayasya saMyuktopaveshanaM sambodhayiSyati|
saMsadi AyavyayavivaraNaM guruvAsare prastoSyate| Ayavyayapatrakasatrasya prathamasatraM pharavarImAsasya
navama dinAkaM yAvat prachliSyati| ekamAsAnantaraM dvitIyabhAgaM mArchmAsasya panJchmadinAt
aprailamAsasya navama dinAGkaM yAvat bhaviSyati|

palsa poliyo auSadham

rASTriyasUchyauSadhadivasAvasare adya panJchvarSato nyUnavayomitebhyaH bAlebhyaH palsa-poliyo
iti auSadhaM pradAsyate| rASTrapatinA rAmanAthakovindena gatadine rASTrapatibhavane ekasmin
kAryakrame asya shubhArambho vihitaH| asmAkaM vArtAhareNa sUchitaM yat asmin kAryakrame
saptadashakoTi bAlebhyaH auSadhaM pradAsyate|

pradhAnamantrI yUeI

pradhAnamantrI narendramodI pharavarI mAsasya navamadinAt‌ dvAdashadvinADakaM॑ bhAvat‌ phalIstIna-
saMyukta-araba-amIrAta-omAna cheti rASTrANAM yAtrAM kariSyati| pradhAnamantriNaH pashchimeshiyA
rASTrANAM yAtrAyAH uddhoSaNAnantaraM saMyukta-araba-amIrAte bhAratIya rAdUtena navadIpasUriNA TvITa
mAdhyamena prokte yat‌ pradhAnamantriNaH abUdhAbI-dubaI nagarayoH dvitIyayAtrayA vyApaka-raNanItika
sahabhAgitAyai vikAsasambaddhasahayogAya ch nUtanakSetreSu gatiH miliSyati|

aphagAnistAne AkramaNam‌

aphagAnistAnasya rAjadhAnyAM kAbulanagaryAM gatadine sanJjAte AtaMkyAkramaNe panJchnavati

AkramaNo'yaM vininditaH| amerikAyAH rASTrapatiH DonAlDaTrampaH AkramaNasya pratyuttarAya
nirNAyakakAryAchraNaM sabalaM  pratipAditavAna saMyuktarASTrasachiva ~ Tilarasana prAvocht‌ yat‌
AtaMkigulmAnAM samarthakAH kathamapi naiva vimoktavyAH|
