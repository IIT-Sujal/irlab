okhI chkravAtaH
okhI chkravAte shanaiH-shanaiH shaithilyam‌ AyAti| gujarAtasya sUrate taTavartikSetre asya
prabhAvaH naiva bhaviSyatiti sambhAvyate| param‌ ahamadAbAdasthitena RtuvinnJAna-vibhAgenoktaM yat‌
dakSiNagujarAte chkravAtasya kinJchita prabhAvaH bhavituM shakyate| AdhikArikasrotobhiH sUchitaM yat‌
tatra rAjye sAhAyyodrArabalAni idAnImapi sannadvAni santi|

gujarAte prachArAbhiyAnam‌

gujarAtavidhAnasabhAnirvAchnAnAM prachArAbhiyAne okhI chkravAtakAraNena
pratikUlaprabhAvaH apatitaH, ataH gatadine neke janasabhAH nirastAH Asan‌| pradhAnamantrI
narendramodI adya dhAndukAyAM dohode netraMge ch nairvAchnika-janasabhAH sambodhayiSyati| samameva
bhAjapAdalasya adhyakSa amitashAhaH kendrIyamantriNaH smRti-irAnI-umAbhAratI-
purUSottamarUpAlA, uttarapradeshamukhyamaMtrI yogI-AdityanAthashch prachArAbhiyAne sahabhAgaM
kariSyanti| kAMgresopAdhyakSaH rAhulagAMdhI api narmadA-dAMga-tApI janapadeSu prachAraM kariSyati|

ambeDakarAya shraddhAMjaliH

mukhyakAryakramaH samAyojayiSyate| yasmin‌ janAH ambeDakarAya shraddhAnJjalim‌ samarpayiSyanti|

pAkistAna saMgharSavirAmaH

pAkistAnena niyantraNarekhAyAM punarekavAraM saMgharSavirAmollaMghanaM kRtam‌| gatasAyaM

pratyuttaraM pradattam‌| bhAratIyapakSato same'pi sainyakarmiNaH surakSitAH vartante|
