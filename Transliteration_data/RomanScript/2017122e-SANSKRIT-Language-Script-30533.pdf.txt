okhI jhanjhAvAtaH

okhI ityAkhyajna jhanjhAvAtanna lakSaddhIpa-pharalyoH atha ch tamilanADu-
rAjyasya dakSiNIya-bhAgaSu prabalA-vRSTiH jAyatA|| lakSaddhIpasya minIkoya-sthalaM
sarvAdhika-duSprabhAvitaM vartata|| bhAratIya-RtuvijnJAna-vibhAgena proktaM yat AgAmiSu
chturvishati-horAsu chkravAto'yaM bhUyo'pi tIvraH bhaviSyati| lakSaddhIpA sImavarti-
 jhanJjhAvAta-kAraNAt sAgarA taraMgAH janayiSyantA adhikAribhiH sUchitaM yat
bhUyo'pi trishata-dhIvarAH uddhatAH santi| avashiSTAnAM kRtAanvaSaNAbhiyAnaM pravartatA
jhanJjhAvAta-kAraNAt tamilanADu-rAjyasya kanyAkumArI-janapadA mahatI kSatiH
sanJjAtA| tatra janajIvanaM paryAkulitaM vartatA|| kanyAkumArI-janapadA mRtakAnAM saMkhyA
samadhya dashamitA sanJjAtA'sti| pradhAnamantrI gatarAtrau tamilanADu-
mukhyamantriNaM palanIsAminaM sambhASitavAn| jhanJjhAvAta- kAraNAt sanJjAta-hAnim
abhilakSya pradhAnamantriNA kSatipUtyartha samAshvAsitam|

nirvAchnAyuktaH-gujarAtam‌

mukhyanirvAchnAyuktasya achlakumAra-jyotivaryasya  nirvAchnAyogasya
dalamekam‌ dvidivasIya-gujarAtayAtrA-prasaMgA adya prAtaH ahamadAbAdaM samprAptam|
dalamidaM rAjyA nairvAchnika-sajjatAyAH sthitiM samIkSiSyati| nirvAchnAyogasya dalaM
vividha-rAjanItika-dalaiH saha sambhASaNaM vidhAya tASAm abhimataM jnJAsyati| shvaH
dalamidaM mukhya -nirvAchnAdhikAriNaM mukhyasachiva variSThAdhikAriNashch milaSyati|



vittamantrI variSTha-bhAjapA-netA ch arUNajaThalI adya abravIt yat‌
vigatashatAbdA navatitama dashakA prashAsananna vivashatA-kAraNAt‌ pariSkAraH pravartitaH
parantu pradhAnamantriNaH modinaH naghutvAdaDhRvishvAsanna pariSkArA pravartyantA adya
gujarAtasya sUrata-nagarA sanJyAramAdhyamaiH saha sambhASaNa-kramA jaThalIvaryeNa proktaM yat‌
ekasmin‌ kAlAdakSApratyakSavaidishaka-nivashaH avarUddhaH AsIt|

jammU kashmIram-mahabUbA

jammU-kashmIrasya mukhyamantrI mahabUbA-muphtI adya sarvasammatyA
shAsanAdhirUDhasya pipulsa-DamlokraTika-pArTIti-dalasya adhyakSA punaH nirvAchitA|
rAjyasya shItakAlika-rAjAdhAnyAM jammUnagaryAm‌ dalasya mukhyAlaya) adyAparAhaNa
nirvAchnaM sampannam| dalasya pravaktA vyajnJApayat yat shrImatImuphtI SaSThaM vAraM
adhyakSapadAya nirvAchitA|

jammU-kashmIram‌ shaityam‌
jammU--kashmIrasya lahasthalaM sarvAdhika-shItAkalita-sthalatvanna pravartatA gatarAtrau
tatra shUnyAd adhaH aSTa-dashamalava-sapta-DigrI-salsiyamitaM tApamAnam abhilikhitam|
