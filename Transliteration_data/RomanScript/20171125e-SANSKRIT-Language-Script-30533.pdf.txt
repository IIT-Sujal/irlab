pradhAnamantrI AhArayojanA

pradhAnamantriNA narendramodinA AhArayojanAnAM pravartanAya kendra
rAjyaprashAsanAni ch samAkAritAni| shrImodI deshe apUrNashArIrikavikAsa-
kupoSaNa-janma samaye nyUnabhAra-raktahInatA sambaddhaprakaraNeSu apichtiH
sabalaM pratipAditavAn|shrI modinA kupoSaNasambaddhasamAsyAnAM nivAraNe
vihitaprayAsAnAM samIkSA kRtA|

rASTrapatiH kurukSetram‌

rASTrapatiH rAmanAthakovindaH adya hariyANAyAH kurukSetre
antArASTriyagItA mahotsavam‌ udghATayat‌| mahotsavo'ya disambaramAsasya
tRtIyadiGnAka yAvat‌ prachliSyati| asmin mahotsave gItA-pAThaH gItA
yajnJH antArASTriyagItA-saMgoSThI shrImadbhagavadgItA-santasammelanaM ch
samAyojayiSyante| morishasa sahayogirASTraM vartate, uttapradeshashch
sahayogirAjyaM vartate|

jammUkashmIra-dineshvarasharmA

jammUkashmIrasthitiviSaye kendrasya visheSapratinidhiH dineshvarasharmA
adya resaI janapadasya tavADAkSetre jammUkSetre visthApitAnAM shivireSu
AtaGvAdaprabhAvita-parivAraiH saha sambhASaNaM kRtavAn| anantaraM ch madhyAhane
rAjanItika-sAmAjika-pratinidhibhiH sAka mititavAn‌|

rASTrapatiH vidhidivasaH

rASTrapatiH rAmanAthakovindaH vAkkIlAn adhyArthayat‌ yat te
nirdhanebhyaH nishulkavidhisevApradadhyuH| adaya navadil‍lyAM
rASTriyavidhidivasAvasare dvidivasIyaM sammelanam udghA|yatA rASTrapatinA
pratipAdita yat‌ samprati nyAyapAlikAyAM mAnavasasAdhanAnAm‌ unnayanasya
AvashyakatA vartate| atrAvasare pradhAna-nyAyAdhIshaH dIpakamishraH avocht‌ yat‌
nAgarikANAM maulikAdhikArANAM sarakSaNa nyAyapAlikAyAH prAthamika-kartavyaM
vartate|

nAyaDUH chlachichitram‌

uparASTrapatiH ema. veMkaiyA nAyaDUH avocht yat hiMsakabhayasya
shArIrikapratADanAyai puraskArodghoSaNAyAshch lokatantre svIkAryatA na vartate|
adya navadillayAm ekasmin sAhityika-kAryakrame tenodIritaM yat deshe
vidhivyavasthAyaiH hAniH naiva sahiSyate| katipayachlachchitrebhyaH
nUtanasamasyAH samudeti| janAnAM dhArmika-sAmAjika bhAvanAyAm‌ AghAtakRte
sati virodhapradarshanAni vidhIyante|

mana kI bAta kAryakramaH

pradhAnamantrI shrInarendramodI shvaH AkAshavANyAH mana kI bAta kAryakrame
nijavichArAn deshavAsinAM purataH prastoSyati| shrImodinA janAH adhyArthitAH
yat te nija-sammarshAn narendramodI epa, mAya gova phorama, ityatra
sampreSayeyuH| janAH nijasammarshAn‌ ekam‌-aSTa-shUnyaM-shUnya-shUnyaM-ekam‌-
ekam‌-sapta-aSA-shanyaM-shUnyaM iti dUrabhaSe niHshulkameva dhyanyaMkanaM kRtvA
praSayitumarhiSyanti| kAryakramo'yaM AkAshavANyA, dUradarshanasya ch sarvAsu
AvRttiSu shrutigamyo bhaviSyati|

gujarAtaprachAraH

gujarAtavidhAnasabhAyAH prathamachraNAtmakamatadAnAya prachArAbhiyAnaM
sajavaM pravartate| nAmAMkanapratyAvartanasya antime dine hyaH vRhat saMkhyAyAm
nAmAMkanapatrANi pratyAvartitAni| avadheyamasti yat disambaramAsasya navame
dinAMke navAshItyAsanebhyaH matadAnaM bhaviSyati| bhAjapA-kAMgresa dalAbhyAM
pramukhaprachArakAH gujarAte samAgachchanti|

krikeTa
