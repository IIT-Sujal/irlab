mukhyavArtAH

 niveshaka sevA dAtrI antArASTriya mAnaka-saMsthayA mUDIj ityanena bhAratasya
sthAnaM vivardhya bI e e- dvi vihitam, anena bhArate niveshaH sthira-shreNyAM
samAgataH|

 prashAsanena mUDIj saMsthayA bhAratasya sthAnasyonnayanaM svAgatIkRtam|
vittamantriNA aruNa jeTalinA uktaM yat idaM bhArate pravartitasya
maulikaparivartanAnAM parichAyakamasti|

 bhAratIya janatA-pArTItidalena gujarAta-vidhAnasabhA nirvAchnAnAM kRte saptatiH
pratyAshinAM prathamA sUchI udghoSitA|

mUDIja

niveshaka-sevA-dAtrI antAraSTiya-mAnaka-sasthA mUDIja ityanayA
ArthikasAMsthAnikasaMskAraiH samadhikavRddhi-sambhAvanadRSTyA bhAratasya sthAnam‌
unnItamasti| bhAratasya sthAnaM bI e e-tri itaH unnIya bI e e-dvi iti kRtam|
anena bhArate niveshasya sthiratA surakSA ch sUchyate| chturdashavarSANi anantaraM bhArate
niveshasthitiH unnitAsti| atrAntare vitta -mantriNA aruNajaTelinA proktaM yat
niveshamAnakasthitiH unnattiH vartamAna prashAsanena kAryAnvita-Arthika
saMskAraprakriyAyAH pariNAmo'sti| vArtAhara-sammelane tenoktaM yat arthavyavasthAyAM
maulika-parivartanAni vihitAni, eteSAM hi pariNAmaH drutavikAsaH vartate, agre ch
bhaviSyati|

sIpIAI-ema mUDIja

mArksavAdI kamyunisTapArTI dvArA mUDIja saMsthayA bhAratasya mAna-saMvardhana
viSaye prashnachihanamutthApitamasti| TvITa-sandeshe dalasya mahAsachivena sItArAma
yechuriNA bhaNitaM yat‌ deshe vigata-tri-chtu-varSebhyaH drutagatyA grAmINadUravasthA-kupoSaNa-
kSudhA-laiMgikabhedabhAvAH ch satataM pravardhante|

sImAramaNaH

pratirakSA mantriNA nirmalA-sItAramaNena rAphAla-sainyavimAnakrayaNe kAngresa-
dalasyAropaH nirAkRtaH| navadillyAM vArtAharaiH sambhASamANayA tayodIritaM yat ete
rAjanaitikAropAH vartante| vimAnAnAM krayaNaprakriyA pUrNarUpeNa vidhisammata-svachcha-
prakriyAm anusRtya vihitA asti| hayaH kAMgresavalena AropitaM yat pradhAnamantrI
ekasya vaNijaH lAbhArthaM sampUrNA prakriyAM parivartitavAn‌ AsIt‌|

bhAjapA gujarAtaH

bhAratIya-janatA-pArTIti-datena gujarAta-vidhAnasabhAyAH nirvAchne saptatiH
pratyAshinAM prathamA sUchchau prakAshitA asti| mukhyamantrI vijayarUpAnI rAjakoTataH
spardhiSyate, upamukhyamantrI nitina-paTelashch mehasANA-kSetrataH pratyAshi-bhavitA| kAMgresa
dalena svIya-pratyAshisUchI naivodughoSitA'sti|

bhArata-pha़Ansa

bhArata-yAtrArataH pha़rAMsadeshIya yUropa-vaideshika-viSayANAM mantrI jyA-yevsa-lI-Dijana
varyaH navadillyAmadya videsha mantriNaM suSamAsvarAjaM militavAn| ubhAbhyAM
pArasparika-hita-prakaraNAni chrchitAni|

jammU-kashmIra-lashkara

jammU-kashmIre saptAha-pUrvaM AtaMki-gulme lashkare-taiyabA-ityasmin samAviSTaH
pAdakanduka-krIDakaH mAjida-ArshidaH surakSA-balAnAM samakSam AtmasamarpaNaM gatarAtrau
kRtavAn‌| tena svashastrAstrANi api samarpitANi|

amerikA-pAka

ekena-amerikIya-shIrSa-senApatinA proktaM yat‌ pAkistAnam‌ kSatre surakSA-
sthairyArtham AtaMkinAM AkramaNahatyAdikAryaM jAtam| svadeshe sImApAreSu ch nivArayet|
amerikA-dUtAvAsasya ekasyAM vijnJptyAM pAkistAnayAtrArataH kamAMDara-janarala losepha-
voTela proktavAna yat pAkistAna-aphagAnistAna yormadhye sambandha bhadrataro bhavet|

bhArata-chIna

sandhArayituM kRtavantau| upadeshane deshe parasparaM vishvAsa-saMvardhanopAyAH -sainya samparka
vivardhanAdi viSayAH chrchitAH| bhArata-chIna-etat parAmarsha-sahayoga-upaveshanaM bIjIga
nagare adya sampannam|
