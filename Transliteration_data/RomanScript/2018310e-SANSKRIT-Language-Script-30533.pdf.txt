pradhAnamantrI

pradhAnamantrI narendramodI deshasya vachitajanAdAM  vikAsAya
kAryAchraNasyAvashyakatAM pratitaditavAna| amunA adya saMsadaH kendrIyakakSe
dvidivasIya-rASTriyavidhAyaka-sammelanaM samudghATitam| tena bhANitaM yat prashAsanena
etAdRk‌  paMchdashAdhika-janAnAM sUchiH prakAshitA ye vikAsaiSTyA tu vaMchitAH santi|
eteSAM janAnAM vikAsena mAnavavikAsasUchkAke bhAratasya sthitiH  edhiSyate|
etad dishi sakArAtmaka-parivartanamAvashyakam‌ vidyate| etat pUrvaM lokasabhAdhyakSA sumitrA
mahAjanaH sAMsadAn vidhAyakAn ch sunishchetuM proktavatI yat‌ kalyANAriyojanAbhyaH
antimavyaktiH lAbhAnvitaH syAt|

vArtA

pradhAnamantriNA narendramodinA proktaM yat bhArata-phrAMsa-sainya-sambandhaH
deshayormadhye rakSA-sahayogayoH aitihAsika-svarNimakAlatvena vidyate| navadilyAmadya
haidarAbAdabhavane phrAMsa-rASTrAtinA imAnuela maikro ityanena sArdhaM samanuSThita-
shiSTamaNDalastarIyoH veshanAnantaraM vArtAharaiH sambhASamANena pradhAnamantriNA etad‌
proktam bhArata-phrAMsayormadhye paramANu-UrjA-rakSA- paryAvaraNAdikSetreSu sahayogasya chturdasha-
sandhi patrANi hastAkSaritAni| etadanantaram ubhayoH pakSataH saMyuktavaktavyamapi
prakAshitam| phrAMsarASTratatinA AtaMkavAdonmUlanAya vishve shAntisaMrvardhanAya ch
sammilyakAryAchraNArthaM bhAratamanurodhitam|

sAibara-yojanA
