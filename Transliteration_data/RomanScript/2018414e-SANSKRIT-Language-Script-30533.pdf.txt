ambeDakara-jayantI

bhArataratna - DA. bhImarAva-rAmajI-ambeDakarasya saptaviMshatyadhikaika-shatamIM
jayantIm upalakSya kRtajnJrASTram adya tasmai shradadhAMjaliM samarpayati|
ekanavatyadhika-aSTAdasha shatatame varSe bhAratIya saMvidhAnasya mukhya-shilpI
bAbAsAheba ambeDakaraH madhyapradeshasya mahUsthale janma alabhat| samprati nagaramidaM
DA. ambeDakara-nagaratvena abhijnJAyate|

svatantrabhAratasya asau prathamo vidhimantrI avartata| avasare'smin
akhile'pi deshe naikeH kAryakramAH samAyojitAH| rASTrapatinA rAmanAtha-kovindena
uparASTrapatinA vekaiyA-nAyaDunA pradhAnamantriNA narendramodinA ch adya prAtaH
saMsatparisare bAbAsAhaba-ambeDakarasya pratimAyAM kusumAnJjaliH samarpitA|
bAbAsAhaba - DA. bhImarAva-ambeDakarasya jayantIm upalakSya samAyojite
kAryakrame sahabhAgam‌ AdAtuM rASTrapatiH rAmanAtha-kovindaH madhyapradeshe Daॉ.
ambeDakarasya janmasthalasya mahU-nagarasya yAtrAM vyadaghAt‌| rASTrapatinA tasmai
kusumAMjaliH samarpitA| avasare'smin sambhASamANena tena proktaM yat samAnatAyAH
adhikAra-pradAnaM bAbAsAhabasya saMvidhAne sarvAdhika-mahatvapUrNa-yogadAnatvena vartate|

ambeDakaraH IrAnI

prasArabhAratI-ambeDakaraH
