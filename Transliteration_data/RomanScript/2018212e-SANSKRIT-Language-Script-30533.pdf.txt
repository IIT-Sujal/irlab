pradhAnamantrI

pradhAnamantrI narendramodI bhArataM vANijyAya ekaH AkarSakaH deshaH iti pratipAditavAn|
shrImodI pashchimaishiyA-khAti-deshAnAM yAtrAyAH antime dine omAnasya rAjadhAnyAM maskata-nagare
saptati-vaNijAM shiSTamaNDalena upaveshane idam akathayat| videsha-mantrAlayIya-pravaktrA
ravIshakumAreNa kathitaM yat‌ pradhAnamantriNA upaveshane bhArata-omanayormadhye vivardhita-sahabhAgitA
prashaMsitA|

karaNa-nagara

jammU-kashmIre shrInagare karaNa-nagara-kSetre adya pratyUSe AtaMkinAM surakSA balAnAM ch madhye
pravartamAne saMgharSe kendrIyArakSitArakSibalasya ArakSI vIragatiM prAptavAn| rAjyasya ArakSI ch
gurUtarAhataH saMjAtaH| vArtAhareNa sUchitaM yat AtaMkavAdinau ArakSi shiviram AkrAntum udyatau
AstAM, tadA ArakSiNA dvAradeshe eva golikAH saMchAlya AtaMkI mAritaH| shivirasya pArshvavarti-
kSetreSu anveSaNe anye'pi AtaMkinaH saMjnJAtAH, tadanu saMgharSaH prArabdhaH, yo sampratyapi prachlati|

rakSAmantrI

rakSA-mantrI nirmalA-sItAramaNaH surakSA-sthitim anvIkSitum adya jammU-kashmIraM
samprAptavatI| rAjye sainya-shivireSu AtaMkyAkramaNAni anu rakSA-mantriNaH eSA yAtrA bhavati|

uchchtama-nyAyAlayaH

 
uchchtama-nyAyAlayaH Atma-raknArthaM shopiyAn‌ golikA-chAlana-prakaraNe mejara-Adityam‌
anyAn‌ sainikAn‌ ch viruddhaya kimapi kAryAchraNaM na kartuM jammU-kashmIra-prashAsanam‌ samAdishat‌
pradhAna-nyAyAdhIshasya dIpaka-mishrasya adhyakSatAyutena pIThena prakaraNe paMjIkRtA-prAthamikI sthagitA
asti| pIThena kendra-jammU-kashmIra-prashAsanayoH dvi-saptAhAbhyantare uttaraM samAhUtam|

vanya-vivaraNam

deshasya vana-kSetreSu aSTa-sahasra-varga-kilomITarataH adhikA-vRddhiH saMjAtA'sti|
paMchdashottara-dvisahasratama-varSApekSayA iyaM vRddhiH pratishatamekA adhikA vidyate| paryAvaraNa mantriNA
DA. harSavardhanena navadillyAmadya vana-saMbaddha-bhAratasya sthiteH gatavarSasya vivaraNaM prakAshitam|
