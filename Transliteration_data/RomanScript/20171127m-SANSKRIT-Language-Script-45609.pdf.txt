pradhAnamantrI-matadAnAni

pradhAnamantrI shrInarendra-modI lokasabhAyAH, rAjya-vidhAnasabhAnAM ch
matadAnAni yugapat‌ samAyojiyatuM punaH prabalAvashyakatAM pratyapAdayat|
gatadivase navadillyAM saMpanne dvi-divasIya-rASTriya-vidhi-divasa-samArohe
aupasaMhArika satraM sambodhayatA tenodIritaM yat pRthak-pRthak punaHpunashch
matadAnAnAM samAyojana! deshasyopari anAvashyako vyaya-bhAraH samApatati|
tenatadarthaM  deshe vyApaka-chrchAyasamAhUtam‌|

gujarAte vidhAnasabhA-nirvAchnArthaM bhAvi-matadAnAnAM dvitIyasya
antimasya ch chraNasya kRte nAmAMkana-patrANi prapUrayitum adya antimo
divaso vidyate| AgAmi-mAsasya chturdashe dinAMke nirdhArite dvitIya-
chraNAtmaka-matadAne tri-navatiH vidhAnasabhA-kSetrANi vyApRtAni bhavitAraH|
atrAntare, tatra matadAnAnAM prathama-chraNasya kRte rAjanaptika-vRndAnAM
narghAchnika-prachArAbhiyAnAni tIvrIbhUtAni| prathame chraNe tatra matadAnAni
AgAmi-mAsasya navame dinAMke nirdhAritAni vidyante|



kAshmIra-samasyAyAH samAdhAnArtham avirata-sambhASaNAya saMprati jammU-
kashmIraM prati preSitaH kendrIya-pratinidhiH shrIdineshvara-sharmA svIya-yAtrAyAH
dvitIye chraNe adya shrInagaraM prayAti| saH tatra dakSiNa-kashmIre shrInagare ch
sthAnIya-yuvakAn, anyAn pratinidhIMshch meliSyati| itaH prAk‌, tena jammU-
nagare divasa-trayamadhyuSya vibhinna pratinidhi-dalaiH sArdhaM saMmatritam|

madhyapradeshaH-mRtyudaNDaH 

madhyapradesha-prashAsanena mahilA-janAn virUdhya aparAdhAn niroddhuM hyaH
aitihAsikaM nirNayaM gRhNatA dvAdasha-paryanta-varSIyANAM bAlAnAM
balAtkArAparAdhasya doSiNo janAn virUdhya mRtyu-daNDasya prAvadhAna-karaH
prastAvaH anumoditaH| etad‌-viSayako nirNayashch gatadivase rAjya-rAjadhAnyAM
bhopAla-nagaryA mukhya-mantriNaH shivarAja-siha-chauhAnasya
adhyakSatAyAmAyojite mantrimaNDalIye mahattavAdhAyini mantraNopa-veshane
gRhItaH| etatprastAvAtmakaM vidheyaka samprati rAjya-vidhAnasabhAyAM
shItakAlike satre svIkRtyarthaM upasthApayiSyate- iti tatratya-vittamantriNA
vijnJApitam‌|

krikeT
