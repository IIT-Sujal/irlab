pradhAnamantrI

pradhAnamantriNA narendra-modinA proktaM yat prashAsanaM deshe yuvA-udyaminaH agre-sartu
bahavovAyAH vidhIyate| namo epa-mAdhyamena adya asheSa-jagataH sTArTaapa-udyamibhiH  navasRjana
kartRbhiH yUnaissaha samvadane shrImodinA proktaM yat sTArTa-apa-samavAyAH deshe laghu-bRhat-
grAmeSvapi jIvanta-kendra-rUpeNa vikasanti| pradhAnamantriNA proktaM yat meka-ina-iMDiyA-samaM
DijAina-ina-iMDiyA api sTArTa-apa samavAyebhyaH mahattvapUrNaM vidyate| tenodIritaM yat
kSetre'smin‌ utkaSTatvamadhigantuM paryApta-dhana-sAhasa-janAnAM yojanaM mahattvapUrNaM vidyate|
pradhAnamantriNA yuvajanebhyaH kRSi-kSetre AmUla-parivartanAya nava-vichAraissaha AhvAnaM kRtam|
tenodIritaM yat prashAsanena iMDiyA-ekshana-plAna-iti nAma sTArTa-apa-yojanApi prArabdhA|
sTArTa-apa-samavAyebhyaH dhana-upalabdhuM prashAsanena dasha-sahasra-koTi-rupyakANAM nidhiH
vinirmitAsti| pradhAnamantriNA chattIsagaDhe udyamibhiH sambhASaNamapi vihitam| itaH prAk tena
pradhAnamantrI AvAsa yojanA, pradhAnamantrI ujvalA yojanA, pradhAnamantrI mudrA yojanA,
lAbhArthibhiH namo epa-mAdhyamena sambhASaNaM kRtam|
