srAila pradhAnamaMtrI

isrAiladeshasya pradhAnamantrI binyAmina-netanyAhU SaD‌ divasIyabhAratayAtrAyai adya
navadillyAm‌ AgamiSyati| videshamantrI suSamAsvarAjaH madhyAhane ikhAilasya pradhAnamantriNA saha

pratinidhistarIya sambhASaNaM bhaviSyati|

uchchtamanyAyAlayaH

bhAratIyavidhijnJpariSadaH saptasadasyIyA-samitiH nyAyAdhIshachtuSTayasya vaktavya-prakaraNaM
samAsAdhayitum‌ adya uchratama-nyAyAlayasya nyAyAdhIshaiH saha upaveshanaM kariSyati| gatadine
bhAratIyavidhijnJpariSadaH adhyakSaH mananamishrA prAvocht‌ yat‌ etAdRsha-prakaraNAni janasamAnyasya
purato naiva sthApanIyAni, yatohi nyAyapAlikA saMvidhAnasya pramukham‌ aGgam‌ vartate|

shrIlaGakA vittamantrI

 

shrIlaGkAyAH vittamantrI maMgalA-samaravIrA gatadine navadillyAM videshamantrI
