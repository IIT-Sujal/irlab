okhI-chkravAtaH

okhI-chkravAtakAraNAt mahArASTre gujarAte ch vRSTirjAyate| atra saMchetanA ch prasAritA
yat mahArASTrasya kSetreSu gujarAtasya ch dakSiNabhAgeSu ch sAmudrika-sthitiH prabhAvitA
vartate| asmAkaM vArtAharaH sUchyati yat saurASTrasya dakSiNIyagujarAtasya taTavartiSu
kSetreSu rASTriya-Apad-uddhAra-mochnabalasya dalAni sannaddhAni santi| pradhAnamantriNA
narendramodinA proktaM yat tena sthiteH anvIkSaNaM satataM kriyate| TvIT saMdeshe
pradhAnamantriNA proktaM yat prabhAvitebhyaH rAjyebhyaH yathAsaMbhavaM sahayogAH pradIyante|

chkravAtakAraNAt saurASTre dakSiNa-gujarAte ch nirvAchna-prachArAbhiyAnaM prabhAvitam|
bhAjapAdalAnusAreNa dalAdhyakSasya tisaH janasabhAH nirastAH|

rAhula gujarAtaH

kaॉgresopAdhyakSaH rAhulagAdhI adya prAvocht yat gujarAte janAH bhAjapeti dalena
asantuSTAH santi yatohi dalena gata-nirvAchnasamaye ghoSitAni AshvAsanAni naiva
prapUritAni| gujarAtasya anjAranagare janasabhAyAmasau prodIritavAn‌ yat‌ matamidaM
rAjyasya janatAyAH abhimataM vartate| tenoktaM yat bhAjapetidalena gata-
vidhAnasabhAnirvAchnasamaye panJchAshallakSAvAsAnAM nirmANaM AshvAsitam AsIt kintu
adhunAvadhi panJchlakSagRhANyapi naiva nirmitAni santi|

uchchtamanyAyAlayaH ayodhyA

uchchtamanyAyAlayaH rAmajanmabhUmi-bAbarI-masjida-vivAdaM phaibruari-mAsasya
aSTame divase shroSyati| ilAhAbAda-uchchnyAyAlayasya vivAdamenamadhikRtya dashAdhika-
dvisahasratamavarSasya nirNayaM virUddhaya trayodasha-yAchikAH vAdashravaNaM pratIkSante|

maiTisaH pAkistAnam

amarIkIya-rakSA-mantriNA jima-maiTisena adhyarthitaM yat pAkistAnaM sImnaH pAre
aphagAnistAne AtaGkavAdinAM AkramaNAni pratiroddhuM prayAsAn shIghratayA kuryAt|
pAkistAne amarIkIya-dUtAvAsasya vijnJptyAm uktaM yat pAkistAnena svakSetre
AtAkavAdibhyaH pradattasharaNasthalAni pidhAtumapi dviguNitaprayAsAH karttavyAH|

shrIlaGkA-nigRhItA

shrIlaGkAyAm sthalasenayA ela.TI.TI.I. ugravAdisaMghaTanena saha saMgharSa- kAle
palAyitAH dvisahasrAdhikAH sainikAH nigRhItAH ye gatamAse pradatta- kSamAdAnAvadhau
samarpaNaM na kRtavantaH| senApravaktrA uktaM yat aSTAviMshati-sahasrAdadhikeSu palAyaka--
pUrvasainikeSu ekonaviMshatyadhika-dvisahasrasainikAH nigRhItAH santi|

gaDakarI

jalasaMsAdhanamantrI nitina gaDakarI abravIt yat vidyut-kSetramiva jalakSetrANAM
vikAsasyAvashyakatA vartate| adya navadillyAM dvitIya-bhArata-jala-
prabhAvashikharasammelane amunA proktaM yat‌ jalasya abhAvaH nAsti samasyA, apitu
janaiH jalasya samuchitopayogaH rakSaNanJch karttavyam| shrI gaDakarI uktavAn yat
bindushaH tathA nAlikA-sinJchnena jalasya vRthAvyayaH nyUnI bhaviSyati|
