pradhAnamantrI vaijnJAnikAH

pradhAnamantriNA narendramodinA deshe anusandhAne navAchAraprayogArtha vaijnJAnikAH
prodIritAH| prakhyAta-bhautikavid‌ prophesara satyendranAtha- bosasya janmautsavam‌ upalakSya
vaijAnikAn‌ sambodhya amunA prokta yat‌ deshasya sAmAjika-Arthika-samasyAnAM
samAdhAnArtham‌ anusandhAna bhavet| sambodhanamida dillItaH kolakAtAM
drishyashravyopakaraNadvArA preSitam| atrAvasare pradhAnamintraNA bhaNitaM yat‌ vijnJAna-
prayogashAlAH, saMsAdhanAnAm‌ upayogashch janasAmAnya-sambaddha-samasyAsamAdhAnArthaM
karttavyam‌|

mumbaI agnikANDam‌

mumbaI-ArakSibalenAdya kamalA-milsa-parisare agnikANDasya sandarbhe
manoranjanashAlAyAH prabandhakau nigaDitau| asmatvAratauharAnusAreNa tau ghaTanAvasare
tatraivAstAm‌ kintu sAhAyyoddhArakArya॒vinaiva kRtvA palAyitavantau| tayoH virodhe
hatyAdi-prakaraNAni pajIkRtAni santi|

jammUkashmIraH
jammUkashmIrasya pulavAmAjanapadastha-lethapurA-kSetre kendrIyArakSitArakSibalAnAM
shivire AtaMkyAkramaNAdanu anveSaNamadya samAptam‌| mRtAtaMkavAdinaH shavaM chAdhigatam‌|
avadheyamasti yat AkramaNe'smin‌ dvau AtaMkinau vyApAditau| balasya
paMchsainikAshch vIragatiM prAptAH Asan‌|

uttarakoriyA saMchetanA

uttarakoriyAdeshasya netrA kima-jauga-una-varyeNa amerikAdeshaH saMchetya
uktamasti yat‌ amerikAdeshaH AkrAmati chet‌ asau paramANu-astrANi prayokSyati|
navavarSAvasare svIyasambodhanaM tena pratipAdita yat‌ nikhilo'pyamerikAdeshaH
uttarakoriyAyAH paramANu-AyudhAnAM gochre vidyate| atrAvasare amunA dakSiNa-
koriyAdeshena sainyaspardhAyAH samAptyarthamapi tena saha vArtArthaM sAhamatyaM prakaTitam|
