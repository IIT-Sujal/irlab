pradhAnamantrI-mana kI bAta

pradhAnamantrI narendramodI AkAshavANyAH 'mana kI bAta' - kAryakrame adya pUrvAhNe
ekAdasha-vAdane svIya-vichArAn prastoSyati| AkAshavANyAH dUradarshanasya ch sarvAbhiH
vAhinibhiH kAryakramo'yaM prasAriSyate| asya kAryakramasya prasAraNAnantaram
AkAshavANyA kSetrIya-bhASAsu prasAraNaM vidhAsyate| kAryakramo'yaM kSetrIya-bhASAsu
sAyam‌ aSTavAdane punaH prasArayiSyate|

jeTalI-arthavyavasthA


vittamantrI aruNa-jeTalI pratyapAdayat‌ yat‌ bhArate samAnAntarArthavyavasthA
yarthAthArthavyavasthAyAH vRhattarA naiva bhavitum‌ arhiSyati| mumbayyAM puraskAra-samArohaM
sambodhayatA shrIjeTalinA proktaM yat‌ jagataH vikasitArthavyavasthAyAH lakSyam‌ adhigantuM
svachchArthavyavasthAyai prakriyA sajjIkRtA'sti|

jeTalI-chidambaram‌

vittamantrI aruNa-jeTalI nyagadat‌ cht‌ jammU-kashmIrasya svataMtratAyAH ut‌ vA
vRhad-svAyattatAH viSaye kAMgresadalasya sthitiH rASTrahita-pratikUlaM vartate| kashmIrasya
janatAyAH svataMtratA-viSaye prAktana-gRhamantrI dvArA vyAhatAM TippaNIm abhilakSya
shrIjeTalI pratyutarati sma| kAMgresa-dalena proktaM yat vichAro'yaM vaiyaktiko'sti na tu
dalasya|
