mukhyavArtAH

 gujarAtavidhAsabhAyAH nirvAchneSu antimachraNArthaM adya prachArAbhiyAna samAptam‌,
matadAnaM guruvAsare bhaviSyati|

 jammU-kashmIre himapAta-janita-bhUskhalanena panJch sainikAH viluptAH santi|

aphagAnistAna-sarvakAreNa islAmika-sTeTa-AtaGki-gulmasya AkramaNaM
viphalIkartuM yojanaikA prastutA'sti|

gujarAta-prachAraH

gujarAte vidhAnasabhA nirvAchnasya antichraNasya kRte prachArAbhiyAnamadya
samAptimagAt‌| adya pradhAnamantrI narendramodI sAmudrikavimAnena ahamadAbAdasya
sAbaramatInadayAH dharoIjalAshaya samprAptavAn‌| tatra hi pradhAnamantriNA ambAjIdevAlaye
pUjA-archnA kRtA| svIye TvIT‌-sandeshe shrImodinA sUchitamasti yat‌ prashAsanaM
jalamArgavikAsAya prayatnarataM vidyate yena tatra-tatra paryaTana-sthalAnA vikAso bhavet‌|
aparatra navanivarvAchita-kAgresAdhyakSeNa rAhulagAndhinA pradhAnamantrI narendramodI tatratyo
mukhyamantrI ch samAropitau yat tau gatadvAvishativarSeSu gujarAte ekapakSIyaM
vikAsArthameva kAryam akurUtAm| ahamadAbAde vArtAharaiH sambhASamANena amunA proktaM
yat sarvebhyo janebhyaH adhikAro na pradattaH| matadAnaM gurUvAsare bhavitA| tatra hi
nirvAchnAyogena rAjanItikadalAni, pratyAshinaH sasthAnAni ch nirdiSTAni yat‌ tAni
shvaH parashvashch matadAnamAlakSya vijnJApanAni mA prasArayeyuH|

jammUkashmIraH himapAtaH

jammUkashmIrasya shrInagare adya himapAto'bhUt‌| gatarAtrau varSAH rAjye
shuSkavAtAvaraNam‌ asamApayan‌| RtuvijnJAnavibhAgena AgAmi chturviMshati-horAsu
mahathimapAtaH anumitaH| shrInagaraM prati rASTriya-rAjamArgaH mugalamArgaH chAvaruddhau|
himapAta-janita-bhUskhalanena tatratyo sainyasthAtrebhyaH panJchsainikAH viluptAH iti
sUchyante| rakSAsrotAMsi sUchyanti yat sAhAyyoddhArakAryANi sajavaM prachAlyante|

shrIlaMkA dhIvarAH

shrIlaMkAyAH nausenayA trayoviMshatiH bhAratIya-dhIvarAH teSAM panJch naukAshch dhRtAH|
ete dhIvarAH shrIlaMkAyAH jalakSetre praviSTAH Asan‌|

aphagAnistAnam‌

aphagAnistAnasya-sainyabalAni islAmika-sTeTa-iti AtaDa़ki gulmasya
uttarIyakSetreSu pravesham AkramaNanJch pratikartu abhinava-yojanA nirmitA asti|
rakSAmantrAlayena vijnJApitaM yat tAni jehAdI-tattvAni rASTriyatAm avigaNayan
yamasadanaM preSayiSyante|

saMyuktarASTraM bhAratIyArthavyavasthA

saMyuktarASTreNa bhAratIyArthavyavasthAyAH vikAsamitiH aiSamaH prAyaH pAdonasapta-
pratishatAt AgAmirvarSe sapAdasaptaM, tatparevarSe ch saptadashamalavachtvAri-pratishataM
bhaviSyati iti anumitA| AgAmi-varSArthaM vishva-Arthika-sthiti-sambhAvanA-vivaraNaM
bhAratasya suSThu-vikAsaH bhaviSyati iti nigaditam|

nepAlaH

nepAle vAmapaMthi-dalaiH saMsada-prAntIya-vidhAnasabhA-nirvAchneSu pUrNabahumatam‌
adhigatam| taiH pratinidhasabhAyAM sapta-vidhAnasabhAsu ch SaT-SaSThIpratishatam
AsanAni vijitAni|

rashiyA-TarkI

rUsa-rASTrapatiH blAdimira-putinaH turki-rASTrapatiM TayIpa-eDaॉganam‌ saMmilya
sIriyAyAM shAnti-vyavasthAyAH sthApanA-viSaye chrchitavAna| somavAsare tukIdeshasya
rAjadhAnyAM etan-melanaM saMjAtam| netRdvayoH saMyukta-vArtAhara-sammelane shrIputinavaryeNa
proktaM yat saH turkIdeshena saha militvA saMyuktarASTrasya tatvAvadhAne sIriyA-deshe
nirvAchnasyAyojanaM navIna-sarvakArasya saMghaTanArtham ch sahayogaM kariSyati|

ऑsTreliyA

ऑsTreliyAdeshe yUropIya-mahadvAti-nAlikA-kendre adya visphoTaH sanJjAtaH|
ghaTanayA janaH mRtaHH aSTAdashashch vraNitAH| visphoTasya kAraNaM na jnJAtam|
