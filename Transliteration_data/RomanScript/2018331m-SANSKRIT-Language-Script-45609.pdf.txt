pradhAnamantrI-haikathaॉn‌

pradhAnamantrI shrInarendra-modI nyagAdIt‌ yad‌ ichchAshaktiH, kriyAshaktishchetyetad‌
dvayaM kasyachid rASTrasya kRte mahattamA sampattivirdyate, api ch, janaiH parivartanAya
sakRnnirNaye vihite sarvamapi sambhavi jAyate| aSTAdashAdhika-dvi-sahasratama-varSIyAyAH
smArTa iNDiyA haikathAn-pratispardhAyAH nirNAyake chkre samavetAn pratibhA-shAlino yuva-
pratibhAgi-janAn gata-sAyaM navadillyAM dRshya-shravya-praNAlikayA sambodhayatA shrImodinA
samudIritaM yat navAchAreSu sAmpratikInAM vaishvika-samasyAnAM samAdhAnasya pUrNaM sAmarthya
virAjate| saH avAdIt yat kasyAshchit samasyAyAH samAdhAnArthaM tanmUlAnveSaNaM,
navachintana-paddhatyA tatprashamanopAyachintanaM chAvashyake staH| atredaM dhyAnAspadaM
vidyate yat smArTa iNDiyA haikathAn-pratispardheyaM yuvakAnAM vishiSya AbhiyAntrikI-
chchAtrANAM mastiSkeSu vibhinnAM sAmAjika-samasyAnAM samAdhAnAya nUnata-chintana-
pravRttiM protsAhayituM prashAsanena prativarSa samagre deshe samAyojyate| asmadIyo vArtAharo
vijnJApayati yad aiSamaH divasa-dvayAtmake etasmin pratibhA-mahAkumbhe deshasya aSThA-
viMshato spardhA-kendreSu Ahatya lakSa-prAyAH pratishAvino yuvakAH bhAgamAdadAnAH

sthirIkRtAn‌ vyavahArAn‌ yathAvad‌ anupAlayituM pratibaddhatAM prakAshitavantau|
