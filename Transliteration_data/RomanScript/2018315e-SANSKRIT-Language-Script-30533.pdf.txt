uchchtamanyAyAlayaH

uchchtama-nyAyAlayena gujarAta-nyAyAlayaH nirdiSTo'sti yat‌ bhAjapAdalAdhyakSasya
putreNa jayashAhena panJjIkArite AparAdhike mAnahAniprakaraNe apraila-dvAdashIM yAvat‌ na kimapi
kuryAditi| jayashAhena samAchAra-vebaphalakaM "da vAyara' tasya patrakArAshch virUddhaya svasamavAya-
viSaye lekha-prakAshanAdanantaraM prakaraNamidaM panJjIkAritamAsIt‌| gujarAtochchnyAyAlayena

prakaraNasya nirasanArthaM da vAyara - ityasya yAchikA pratyAkhyAtA AsIt‌

rASTrapatiH

rASTrapatiH rAmanAthakovindaH sambhASate yat bhArata-meDAgAskara iti deshau kRSi-
khAdya-prasaMskaraNa-svAsthyAdikSetreSu sammilya kAryaM kartum arhataH| shrIkovindaH adya
meDAgAskara-vishvavidyAlaye uktavAn yat deshayoH prAkRtika-khanija-sampadaH vartante, teSAM
sadupayogena nAgarikAnAM kRte vRttyavasarAH bhaviSyanti|

saMsada

vibhinnaviSayeSu vapikSidalAnAM kolAhalavashAt sadanayoH kAryajAtam adyApi
sthagitam| lokasabhAyAH kAryajAtam AdivasaM sthagitam| rAjyasabhAyAH kAryajAtaM prathamaM
trivAdanaM yAvat tadanu ch shvaH yAvat sthagitam| lokasabhA sthaganAt pUrvaM chrchAM vinaiva grechyuTI
pradAna-saMshodhana-vidheyakam svyakarot| vidheyakena sevakebhyaH kRtajnJtA-dhana-rAshiH dashalakSebhyaH

pradhAnamantrI

pAkistAnam‌

vraNiteSu chturdashArakSiNaH santi| pUrva-pradhAnamantriNaH navAja sharIpha ityasya AvAsaM nikaSA
