pradhAnamantrI

pradhAnamantriNA narendramodinA proktaM yat sAmAjika-surakSA-yojanAbhiH jana-
sAmAnyAnAM jIvane anishchitatA-nivAraNe sAhAyyaM avApti| dRshya-shravya-chAyAGkana-mAdhyamena
prashAsanasya sAmAjika-surakSA-saMbaddha-yojanA-lAbhArthibhissamaM sambhASaNe shrImodinoktaM yat
prashAsanaM sAmAjika-surakSAyAH triSu mahatvapUrNa-prakaraNeNu vishiSTaM-dhyAnavadadAti| tena bhaNitaM
yat pradhAnamantrI surakSA-bImA-yojanA, pradhAnamantrI-jIvana-jyoti-bImA-yojanA, aTala-
peMshana-yojanAbhiH triSu varSeSu viMshati-koTyadhika-janAH lAbhAnvitAH| tena bhaNitaM yat
prashAsanaM nirdhanebhyaH saMvedanashIlaM vartate| teSAM sashaktikaraNAya ch prayAsarataM vidyate| tena bhaNitaM
yat chturdashottara-dvi-sahasratame varSe vaiMkeSu dvi-panJchAshat- tamAH baiMkIya lekhAgArAH abhavan| param
adhunA vivardhya ashIti-pratishanmitAH jAtAH| eteSu mahilAnAM- baiMkIya lekhAgArANAM saMkhyA
api edhitA jAtA|

rASTrapatiH

rASTrapatiH rAmanAtha-kovindaH proktavAn yat prashAsanaM sUkSma-laghu-madhyamodyogebhyaH
adhika-RNa-sahAyatA-dhana-kusIda-shaithilye- navAchAreSu ch vishiSTaM dhyAnavadadAti| saMyukta
rASTra- sUkSma-laghu-madhyamadivasAvasare navadillyAm‌ “udyama-saMgamasya oddhATanike satre tena
bhaNitaM yat‌ nUtana-saMhitAyAM ete udyogAH visheSa-sthAnam‌ adhigatavantaH| avasare'smin‌ tena
grAmINa-bhArate vRtyavasarAH upapAdayituM prAshAsanika-mahatvapUrNa-kAryakramaH “mishana-saura-
chrakhA" ityapi prArabdhaH| ekam‌ ema.esa.ema.I. iti samparka-paTalamapi prArabdham|

 
jhArakhaMDa

jhArakhaMDe naksalibhiH vihite AkramaNe sapta-ArakSiNaH hutAtmabhUtAH| naksalibhiH
gaDa़vA janapade visphoTaka-suraMge visphoTaH kRtaH| upamahAnirIkSakeNa vipula-shuklA varyeNoktaM
yat‌ janapadasya biMjo-kSatre kechn‌ mAovAdinAM sUchnAdhigatA|

uchchtama-nyAyAlayaH

uchchtama-nyAyAlayenoktaM yat‌ dvi-jI-spaikTram-sambaddha-prakaraNAni saMvedanashIlAni
vartante| prakaraNasyAsya anvIkSaNaratAnAm adhikAriNAM pratirupamapi spaSTaM bhavitavyam| shIrSa-
nyAyAlayena eyarasela-maiksisa-prakaraNAnvIkSaNArataM pravartana-nideshAlayasya adhikAriNaM
virUddhaya shravaNakAle eSA vyavasthA prasAritA| kendra-pakSataH atiriktena mahAdhivaktrA
vikramajIta-banarjI varyeNa nyAyAlayaH sUchitaH yat prashAsanaM Ayato'dhika-sampatti-sandhAraNa
adhikAriNAm anvIkSaNaM kartu samIhate sma|


bipina rAvataH

senA pramukheNa bipina rAvatena kashmIra prakaraNe saMyukta-rASTra-mAnavAdhikAra-vivaraNaM
nirAkRtam‌, proktaM yat‌ bhAratIya senA mAnavAdhikAraNAM mAnadaNDAnusAreNa kAryamAchrati|
navadillyAM sAibara-pravidhi-sammelane tenoktaM yat‌ kashmIrarAjye senAyAH kAryaM
pakSapAta-rahitaM-pAradashiM ch vidyate| shrI rAvatena sAibara-yuddhapraNAlyA viSaye proktaM yat‌ chIna
deshaH pravidhi-kSetre agragaNyaH jAtaH| asmAbhiH api kSetre'smin‌ kSamatAM vivardhitavyA|

bhArata-aphagAnistAnaH

bhAratadeshena kathitaM yat‌ aphagAnistAna AtaMkavAda-samasyA sthAnIyA nAsti| tatra
vihitAni AkramaNAni prativeshi-deshaiH vidhIyante, saMyukta-rASTe bhAratasya sthAyi-pratinidhinA
rAjadUtena saiyada-akavabarUhInena suraknA-pariSadi aphagAnistAna-sthitau chrchAvasare idaM proktam|
tena bhaNitaM yat‌ aphagAna-prashAsana-pakSataH shAnti-prastAvAnantaramapi tAlibAnena AkramaNAni
vihitAni, janAH ch nihatAH| aphagAnistAne samparka-vyApAra-nivesha kSetre sahabhAgi-rItyA
bhAratasya sahAyatAM viSaye akabarUhInenoktaM yat‌ jalapotrasthAtre-anekeSu ch kAryeSu bahu-pragatiH
