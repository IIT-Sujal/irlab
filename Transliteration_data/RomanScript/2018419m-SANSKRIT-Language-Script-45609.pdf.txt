pradhAnamantrI - bhArata kI bAta

 
pradhAnamantrI narendramodI nyagadat yat AtaMkavAdaH manAgapi naiva
sahiSyate atha ch AtaMkavAda-niryAtakartRdeshAH samuchitaM pratyutariSyante|
SoDashottara dvisahasratama-varSIyaM sarjikala-sTrAika iti lakSyakendrItAkramaNaM
sandarbhayan‌ shrImodI abrabIt yat bhArataM shAntau vishvasiti paranJch
AtaMkavAda-poSaka-deshAn virudhya kaThoratayA pratyuttariSyati|

pradhAnamantrI gatarAtrau laMDana-nagare bhArata kI bAta sabake sAthaH iti
kAryakrame abhibhASate sma| deshe bAlAbhiH saha duSkarmaghaTanA-viSaye tena duHkhaM
prakatitam‌| tena vyAhRtaM yat etadAdRshAH ghaTanAH na kevalaM vaiyakiti-
durAchArAHapitu sAmAjika-vikRti rUpeNa vartante| etAsAM ghaTanAnAM viSaye
chintAM prakaTayatA pradhAnamantriNA proktaM eteSu viSayeSu rAjanIti naiva
vidheyA| pradhAnamantriNA vyAhataM yat mandagatika-parivartanakAlaH samAptiM
gataH| kendre bhAjapA-dalasya netRtva-yutasya prashAsanasya kAryakAle janAH
samadhika-mahatvAkAMkSI sanJjAtAH| pradhAnamantrI vyAharat yat AyuSmAna-
bhArata-yojanAyAH antargataM bhUyo'pi dasha-koTi-mitAH nirdhana-kuTumbAH
sammilitAH bhaviSyanti|

pradhAnamaMtrI - dvipAkSikasambhASaNam

rASTramaNDala-shAsanAdhyakSANAM sammelanAt prAka pradhAnamantrI narendramodI
gatadine landananagare briTenadeshasya pradhAnamantriNA 'TerejA me' ityanayA sAkaM
dvipAkSikaM sambhASaNaM kRtavAn| briTena-pradhAnamantriNA saha sarvairapi deshai
sammukhIkriyamANaH AtaMkavAda-viSayaH savistaraM parichrchitaH| vijnJAna-pravidhi-
navAchAra chetyAdi viSayAH visheSeNa parichrchitAH|

pradhAnamantrI-sandhiH

bhArata-briTena deshayoH madhye antarjAlIya-sambandha-gaMgA punaruddhAra-
kaushalavikAsa-kSetrANi sametya dasha-sandhi-patrANi hastAkSaritAni|
bhAratIyAyurvijnJAnasaMsthAnena pAramparikAyurveda-kendraM sthApayiSyate|

pradhAnamantrI-chogamaH

pradhAnamantrI narendramodI adya laMDana-nagare rASTaramaNDala-deshAnAM
shAsanAdhyakSANAM sammelane sahabhAgaM kariSyati| asya sammelanasya
viSayo'sti bhaviSyaM prati| davaivArSike sammelane'smin‌ triSanJchAshat-
rASTramaNDala-deshAnAM pratinidhayaH sammilitAH bhaviSyanti|
