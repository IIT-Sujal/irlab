pradhAnamantrI-kRSakaH

pradhAnamantriNA shrInarendra-modinA adya proktaM yad dvAviMshattyuttara-dvi-sahasra-tama-varSe
yAvat kRSakANAm AyaM dviguNIkarttu kRSikSetrasya mahAyavyayapatrakaM dviguNIkRtya dvi-lakSa-koTi-
rupyakato'pyadhikAnAm‌ AvaNTanaM kRtamasti| 'namo epa' iti chriSNutantra-mAdhyamena kRSakaiH
saha sambhASaNAvasare pradhAnamantriNA proditaM yad vigata yU. pI. e. prashAsanasya
shAsanakAlApekSayA ena. DI. e. prashAsanena kRSikSetrAya dviguNitAni rupyakANi AvaNTitAni
santi| shrImodinA bhaNitaM yat‌ kRSakebhyaH teSAmutpAdasya sArdhaika-guNitamadhikaM dhanam‌
upapAdayituM aSTAdasha-ekonaviMshatyuttara-dvi-sahasakharatama-varSIya-Ayavyayapatrake prAvadhAnaM
vihitamAsIt‌ pradhAnamantriNA prodIritaM yat‌ prashAsanam‌ apariSkRta-vastunaH mUlyApachyane,
utpAdAnAM samuchita-mUlyopapAdane, utpAda-vinAshasyAvarodhane, kRSakebhyaH Ayasya vaikalpika-
sAdhanopapAdane ch savisheSaM dattAvadhAnaM vartate| shrImodinA sabalaM vyAhataM yat‌ prashAsanaM
kRSakebhyaH sarvadA sarvathA ch sAhAyyArthaM kAryamAchrati| anenoktaM yat‌ kRSakebhyo mRdA svAsthya-
patrANyapi upapAditAni santi yena kRSakAH mRdAyAH poSakattatvAni prati jAgarUkAH
sachetanAshch bhaveyuH| uchchstarIya-bIjAnAM krayaNArthaM kRSakebhyaH RNamapi pradIyate| yugapadeva

nirmalA-sItAramaNaH

rakSAmantriNA nirmalA sItAramaNa-varyayA adya jammU-kashmIre punJcha janapade hutAtmano
bhaTasya auraMgajebasya paitRka-grAme tasya pArivArika-janaiH samaM melanaM kRtam| tayA bhaTasya
parivArAya sarva-saMbhava-sahAyatA-pradAnam AshvAsitam| gata-saptAhe AtaMkibhiH Ida parvaNi gRhaM
gamyamAnam auraMgajebam apahRtya tasya hatyA kRtA AsIt|

rASTrapatiH rAmanAtha-kovindaH grIsa, sUrInAma, kyUbA cheti deshatrayasya yAtrAyAH dvitIye
chraNe sUrInAma-rAjadhAnIM “pArAmAribo' nagarIM samprAptavAn‌| tatratyena rASTrapatinA “deshera
dalAno botarashaa” ityamunA vimAnapattane rASTrapateH bhavyaM svAgataM kRtam| shrIkovindaH adya
tatratyena rASTrapatinA samaM dvaipAkSikaM sambhASaNaM vidhAsyati| tatashch shiSTamaNDala-starIya-
sambhASaNaM saMsAdhya deshadvayena bhUri sAhamatya-patrANi api hastAkSarayiSyante

aphagAnistAnasya 'badagIsa' prAnte adya prabhAte sainyabalasya ekasmin sthAtre
