rAjanAthaH-jammU-kashmIraH

kashmIra-viSaye sambhASaNAnuSTAnArthaM guptachra-vibhAgIya-pUrva-nideshakasya dineshvara-
sharmaNo niyukti-viSayakaH kendra-shAsakIyo nirNayaH sakalairapi rAjanaitika-dalaiH svAgatI-
kRto'sti| jammU-kashmIrasya mukhyamantrI mehavUbA muphtI avadat‌ yattatra agra-saraNArthaM sambhASaNaM
sarvathA Avashyakam‌, ekalo mArgashch vidyate| gatasAyaM navadillyAM sandarbhe'smin‌ vArtAharAn‌
vijnJApayatA gRhamantriNA rAjanAtha-siMhena proktaM yat prashAsanaM kAshmIra-samasyAyAH
samAdhAnArthaM sthira-saMvAdam abhivAnJchati|

himAchla-pradeshe bhAvi-vidhAnasabhA-nirvAchnArthaM vibhinnaiH pratyAshibhiH gatadivasa-
paryantaM prapUritAni SaT-saptatyadhika-chtushshataM nAmAMkana-patrANi adya parIkSiSyante| hyaH antime
divase pAdona-trishataM nAmAMkana-patrANi prAptAni| atredaM dhyAnAspadaM vartate yad aSTa-SaSTi-
sadasyAtmikAyAH himAchla-pradesha-vidhAnasabhAyAH nirvAchnArthaM matadAnAni AgAmi-mAsasya
navame dinAMke nirdhAritAni vidyante|

shIrSanyAyAlayaH-nirvAchnAyogaH-dalAni

shIrSa-nyAyAlayena nirvAchnAyogo nirdiSTaH yadasau nirvAchna-krame avaidha-dhanAnAM prabalaM
duSprayogamavaroddhuM rAjanaitika-dalAnAM sUchnAdhikArAdhiniyamasya paridhau AnayanArthaM kanJchna
vishiSTa-prastAvaM parIkSeta| sandarbhe'smin nyAyakhaNDa- pIThena prastAvopasthApakaH dillI-

Tilarsan‌-bhAratam‌

amarikIyo videsha-mantrI reks‌ ublyU.Tilarsan‌ avadat‌ yad‌ bhAratena sArdham‌
amerikAyAH mahat vAdhAyi-raNanaitika-sambandho na khalu dakSiNaishiyA-paryantameva
parisImito'sti| gatadivase svIya-Akasmika-yAtrAyAm‌ aphagAnistAna saMprAptaH Tilarsan‌
avAdIt‌ yat‌ Tarampa-prashAsanaM shAnti-sthairya-yutasya aphagAnistAnasya vinimaNi bhArataM nitarAM
mahattvapUrNA guNAtmikAM ch bhUmikAM nirvodumarhati| Tilarsan‌ pAkistAnasya yAtrAnantaram‌ adya
tri-divasIya-bhArata-yAtrAyAM navadillIM saMprApnuvAno vidyate|
