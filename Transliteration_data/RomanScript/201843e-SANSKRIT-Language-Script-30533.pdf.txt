uchchtama-nyAyAlayaH

uchchtama-nyAyAlayaH anusUchita-jAti-janajAti atyAchAra
niyantraNAdhiniyamamAshritya sva-nirNayaM na sthagitavAn| adya kendrasya punarvichAra-
yAchikAyAH shravaNe nyAyAlayenoktaM yat sA yAchikA savistaraM shroSyate| yAchikA
dasha-dinAni anantaraM punaH shroSyate| nyAyAlayena tAvat sarve pakSAH sva-sva-
likhitottaraM pradAtum adhyarthitAH| mArch-viMshatyAM pradatta-nirNaye nyAyAlayena
vyavasthA-pradattAsIt yat tad-vidherantargataM kasyApi svataH nigrahaNaM na shakyamiti|
